<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-19832 is  my childhood friend, Laura</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-19832<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-19832</i> is a humanoid entity resembling Laura, a childhood friend of the researcher who discovered it. It appears to be in its late twenties and wears clothing consistent with the fashion trends at the time of its discovery.<br><br><i>SCP-19832</i> is sapient and capable of communication, though it speaks in an archaic dialect that has been difficult to understand. Its primary motivation seems to be social interaction; it often attempts to engage with personnel in conversation or activities such as card games or board games.<br><br>When not engaging in conversation or activities, <i>SCP-19832</i> will typically remain motionless for extended periods of time before resuming its previous behavior patterns. It does not appear to require sustenance and shows no signs of aging since its initial discovery.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br><i>SCP-19832</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. Personnel are not to interact with <i>SCP-19832</i> without prior approval from the Site Director. Any attempts by personnel to engage in conversation or activities with <i>SCP-19832</i> should be reported immediately.<br><br>In order to better understand its behavior, researchers are encouraged to observe and document any changes in its behavior patterns over time.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19832.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-19832</i> was observed engaging in a conversation with the researcher who discovered it. How about yourself?<br><b><br><i>SCP-19832</i>:</b> I'm doing alright. It's nice to be able to talk to someone again after all this time.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19832.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-19832</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Interviewee:</b> <i>SCP-19832</i><br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Hello, Laura.
