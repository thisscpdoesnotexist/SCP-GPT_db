The entity does not appear to have any physical form, but it can interact with its surroundings through various methods such as sound manipulation and telepathy.<br><br><i>SCP-17141</i> has been observed to exhibit a range of emotions and behaviors, ranging from playful and curious to hostile and aggressive. It is capable of manipulating sound waves in order to create music or speech, although it rarely speaks directly with humans unless provoked or threatened. When angered, <i>SCP-17141</i> has been known to emit loud screeching noises that can cause extreme discomfort for those nearby.<br><br>When interacting with other entities or objects, <i>SCP-17141</i> often displays manipulative behavior in order to achieve its desired outcome; this includes using its telepathic abilities in order to influence people's mindsets or manipulate their behavior.
