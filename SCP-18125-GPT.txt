<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18125 is  the seven secret names of God.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18125<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18125</i> is a set of seven secret names of God, which are believed to have immense power. Each name has been inscribed in an ancient language on a separate sheet of paper, with the words written in gold ink and surrounded by intricate designs.<br><br>When any one of the names is spoken aloud, it will cause an anomalous effect that varies depending on the context and intent behind its use. The effects range from minor changes to reality (such as altering physical objects or temporarily altering the laws of physics) to more powerful events such as summoning entities or granting wishes.<br><br>The exact nature and extent of these effects are unknown, but they appear to be related to the speaker's level of faith and understanding when using <i>SCP-18125</i>. It is also possible that some individuals may possess greater control over these powers than others due to their spiritual connection with God.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br><i>SCP-18125</i> is to be kept in a secure containment locker at Site-██. Access to <i>SCP-18125</i> is restricted and requires Level 5 clearance. Any personnel wishing to study or use <i>SCP-18125</i> must submit a request for permission, which will be reviewed by the Site Director before being granted.<br><br>Any attempts to use <i>SCP-18125</i> without proper authorization are strictly forbidden and may result in disciplinary action.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18125.1: </h3><br><br>The following is a list of the seven names contained within <i>SCP-18125</i>:<br><br>• El Shaddai - The Almighty God.<br>• Adonai - Lord and Master.<br>• YHVH - The Unutterable Name of God. <br>• Ehyeh Asher Ehyeh - I am that I am. <br>• Elohim - God in Three Persons. <br>• Yahweh Sabaoth – Lord of Hosts. <br>• Tetragrammaton – The Four Letters of God's Name<br><br><h3> Addendum 18125.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-18125</i><br><br>On ██/██/20██, Dr. ███████ conducted an interview with <i>SCP-18125</i> to gain further information about its powers.
