<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><i>SCP-9003-GPT</i> is  a compass that points to the nearest fan of Lady Gaga.<br><br><h5>Item #:</h5> <i>SCP-9003-GPT</i><br><br><h5>Object Class:</h5> Safe<br><br><h5>Special Containment Procedures:</h5>  <i>SCP-9003-GPT</i> is to be kept in an opaque box in Storage Site-45. SCP-9003 is not to be opened unless testing by a level 3 personnel.<br><br><br><h5>Description:</h5> <i>SCP-9003-GPT</i> is a compass. The needle of SCP-9003 points to the nearest fan of Lady Gaga. When pointed at fans of Lady Gaga, the needle moves in a circular motion and once it points to one it will point towards another fan in an endless cycle until pointed at something else.<br><br>SCP-9003 was found on ██/██/20██ after reports were coming in from ██████, Illinois about people hearing "Bad Romance" by Lady Gaga on repeat constantly while outside their house for no apparent reason with no record of said song being played in that area or time. After investigation by Foundation officials and the police force there, they discovered that all those affected had their houses completely filled with various memorabilia relating to Lady Gaga's career as well as posters or pictures depicting her face on them. It was then discovered that all those affected were also fans of her music which led to discovery of SCP - 9003 - GPT . The subjects claimed they had not been listening to any music before this event but when questioned about how they did not notice it happening, several said "I don't know how I didn't find out sooner." All subjects were administered Class B amnestics and released shortly after this incident.<br><br><h5>Recovery:</h5> SCP-9003 was discovered by Agents S█████ and P█████ after they heard about the incident in ██████, Illinois. They were assigned to go there and find out what was going on. After questioning several people who lived in the house where all these incidents happened, it was discovered that a lady named F████████ was living there at the time with her daughter C██████████ after she got divorced from her husband. One of them said "She never really liked Lady Gaga but one day it just happened." <i>SCP-9004-GPT</i> was found in a box inside C██████████'s room covered in stickers of Lady Gaga as well as several posters depicting her on them. She claimed that she hated this compass but also said "It's not like I would throw it away." When questioned about how this compass ended up being placed inside her room she said "I don't know how it got here." Class A amnestics were given to those affected by SCP-9003 - GPT .<br><br><br><h5>Addendum 1:</h5><br><br><h5>Addendum 2:</h5></div>
