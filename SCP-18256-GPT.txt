<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18256 is  a man who has the same exact voice as Site Director Gabe Brown. SCP-18256 claims to be "a voice actor from the real world" who "voices Gabe Brown in the Netflix SCP Foundation Cartoon Series."</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18256<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18256</i> is an adult human male, approximately 40 years of age, who has the same exact voice as Site Director Gabe Brown. <i>SCP-18256</i> claims to be <i>"a voice actor from the real world"</i> who <i>"voices Gabe Brown in the Netflix SCP Foundation Cartoon Series."</i><br><br>When questioned further about his identity and purpose, <i>SCP-18256</i> becomes evasive and refuses to answer any questions. He does not appear to have any knowledge of or interest in the Foundation or its activities.<br><br>In addition to having a similar vocal pattern and inflection as Site Director Gabe Brown, <i>SCP-18256</i> also appears to possess some degree of telepathic ability when communicating with other people. When speaking with individuals he has never met before, he is able to accurately predict their reactions and responses without prompting or prior knowledge.<br><br>The source of this ability is unknown at this time. It is possible that it may be related to his anomalous nature; however further testing will need to be conducted before any conclusions can be drawn.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18256</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. He is to be provided with three meals per day and allowed access to recreational activities as approved by the site director.<br><br>Personnel assigned to <i>SCP-18256</i> are not permitted to ask him any questions about his identity or purpose, nor are they allowed to make any attempts at communication beyond basic instructions and requests. Any personnel found violating these rules will be subject to disciplinary action.<br><br>In order to prevent <i>SCP-18256</i> from using his anomalous abilities on personnel, all conversations with him must take place over video conference or other audio/visual mediums that do not allow for direct physical contact between the two parties.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18256.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-18256</i> was observed to have a conversation with Site Director Gabe Brown. During this conversation, <i>SCP-18256</i> revealed that he had been sent from an alternate universe in order to <i>"test the boundaries of reality"</i> and <i>"see what happens when two identical people meet."</i><br><br>After the conversation concluded, <i>SCP-18256</i> immediately ceased all attempts at communication and has not spoken since.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18256.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-18256</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████████<br><b><br>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-18256</i><br><br>&ltBegin Log&gt<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> Good evening, <i>SCP-18256</i>.
