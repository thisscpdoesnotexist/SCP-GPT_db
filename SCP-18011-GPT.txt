<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18011 is  conch shell that can steal voices and be used to imitate them</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18011<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18011</i> is a conch shell of unknown origin measuring approximately 8 cm in length and 5 cm in width. The outer surface of the shell is decorated with various symbols, including a stylized human face, which are believed to be of Polynesian origin.<br><br>When any living creature speaks into <i>SCP-18011</i>, their voice will be absorbed by the object. After this occurs, it can then imitate the voice of the speaker with perfect accuracy for up to one hour before reverting back to its original state.<br><br>In addition, <i>SCP-18011</i> appears to possess some degree of sentience and has been observed responding to verbal commands from individuals who have spoken into it previously. It is unclear what type of intelligence <i>SCP-18011</i> possesses or how it is able to respond in such a manner; further testing is required.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18011</i> is to be kept in a secure locker at Site-██. Access to the object must be approved by personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Under no circumstances should any personnel attempt to speak into <i>SCP-18011</i> without authorization from a senior staff member. Any unauthorized attempts will result in disciplinary action.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18011.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-18011</i> was recovered from a private residence in [REDACTED], after reports of an individual using the object to impersonate local law enforcement officers. The individual in question has been apprehended and is currently being held for questioning.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18011.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-18011</i><br><br>On ██/██/20██, Dr. ██████ attempted to communicate with <i>SCP-18011</i> using a voice recorder. The following is an excerpt from the interview:<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> “Hello? Can you hear me?”<br><b><i>SCP-18011</i>:</b> “Yes, I can hear you. Who are you?” <br><b>Dr. ██████:</b> “My name is Dr. ██████ and I am here to ask you some questions about yourself and your abilities.” <br><b><i>SCP-18011</i>:</b> “I understand… what would you like to know?”<br><br>Dr. ██████ proceeded to ask <i>SCP-18011</i> a variety of questions regarding its origin, capabilities, and sentience. The interview concluded after approximately 20 minutes with no new information being gained.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18011.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Date:</b> ██/██/████<br><b>Subject:</b> D-9012, a male human subject.<br><b>Procedure:</b> D-9012 was instructed to speak into <i>SCP-18011</i> for one minute. After this time had elapsed, the object was observed to have absorbed his voice and began responding to verbal commands from the subject. The following is a transcript of the experiment.<br><b><br>D-9012:</b> <i>"Hello?"</i> <br><i>SCP-18011</i> (imitating D-9012's voice): <i>"Yes?"</i> <br><b>D-9012:</b> <i>"Can you repeat what I say?"</i> <br><i>SCP-18011</i> (imitating D-9012's voice): <i>"Yes."</i> <br><b>D-9012:</b> <i>"What color is the sky?"</i> <br><i>SCP-18011</i> (imitating D-9012's voice): <i>"The sky is blue."</i></div>
