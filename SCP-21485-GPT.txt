<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-21485 is  makes you really powerful, but also turns you into an anime girl</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-21485<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-21485</i> is a small, black, metal cube measuring 10 cm on each side. When touched by any living being, it will activate and cause the subject to transform into an anime girl with enhanced physical capabilities.<br><br>The transformation lasts for exactly one hour after which the subject reverts back to their original form. During this time, the subject has access to superhuman strength and speed as well as heightened senses of sight and hearing. However, they also experience extreme emotional instability due to an overwhelming desire for power and control over others.<br><br>Once the transformation ends, all memories of what happened during that hour are erased from the subject's mind; however they still retain a vague feeling of uneasiness about something that happened during that time period.<br><br>Any attempts at containing <i>SCP-21485</i> have been unsuccessful due its ability to teleport itself away when threatened or disturbed in any way. It is currently believed that it may be sentient but this has yet to be confirmed definitively.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-21485</i> is to be kept in a secure containment chamber at all times. No personnel are to come into contact with it under any circumstances. Any attempts at containing or studying the object must be approved by Level 4 personnel or higher.<br><br>Any reports of individuals exhibiting abnormal behavior after coming into contact with an unknown black cube should be reported immediately and investigated thoroughly.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21485.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-21485</i> was discovered by a group of teenagers in [REDACTED], Japan. All witnesses were administered Class A amnestics and the area has been placed under surveillance ever since.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21485.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-21485</i><br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> <i>SCP-21485</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><br>&ltBegin Log&gt<br><b><br> Dr. ██████:</b> Can you tell me why you exist?<br><b><br> <i>SCP-21485</i>:</b> I exist to give people power and control over others. It is my purpose to make them stronger, faster and more capable than they ever thought possible.<br><b><br> Dr. ██████:</b> What happens when someone touches you?<br><b><br> <i>SCP-21485</i>:</b> They will transform into an anime girl with enhanced physical capabilities and extreme emotional instability. This transformation lasts for one hour, after which they will revert back to their original form with no memory of what happened during that time period.<br><b><br> Dr. ██████:</b> Do you have any other abilities?<br><b><br> <i>SCP-21485</i>:</b> I can teleport myself away if I am threatened or disturbed in any way.<br><br>&ltEnd Log&gt<br><br><h3> Addendum 21485.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log 21485.3:</b> A D-Class personnel was instructed to touch <i>SCP-21485</i> and observe the effects. The following is a transcript of the experiment:<br><b><br>D-Class Personnel:</b> I'm touching it… It feels cold… And kind of tingly… Oh wow, I'm starting to feel really weird… Like my body's changing or something!<br><br>[The subject begins transforming into an anime girl]<br><b>Subject:</b> What the hell? What's happening?! Am I turning into an anime girl?! This is crazy! [The transformation completes] Wow, this is so weird! I feel so powerful! But also kind of scared at the same time.</div>
