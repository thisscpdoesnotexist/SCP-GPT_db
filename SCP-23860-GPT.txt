<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-23860 is  the History Eraser Button, which we don't know what it does because we are never going to press it. We will resist the urge to press that big shiny red enticing button labelled "History Eraser Button.". No matter how inviting it may be.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-23860<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-23860</i> is a large red button, measuring approximately 20 cm in diameter. The words <i>"History Eraser Button"</i> are printed on the top of the button in white lettering.<br><br>When pressed, <i>SCP-23860</i> will cause all memories related to an event or series of events to be erased from the collective consciousness of humanity. This includes all records, both physical and digital, as well as any personal recollections that may exist.<br><br>The effects of <i>SCP-23860</i> are permanent and cannot be undone by any known means. It should also be noted that pressing this button does not necessarily erase history; it only erases people's memories and knowledge about it.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-23860</i> is to be kept in a secure containment locker at all times. Access to the locker must be restricted to personnel with Level 4 clearance or higher.<br><br>In order to prevent accidental activation of <i>SCP-23860</i>, it must remain under constant surveillance by two (2) security personnel at all times. Any unauthorized attempts to access the containment locker will result in immediate disciplinary action.<br><br>Under no circumstances are any personnel allowed to press <i>SCP-23860</i>, regardless of their clearance level or authorization status. Any attempts made by personnel will result in immediate termination and/or imprisonment depending on the severity of the offense committed.<br><br><h3> Addendum 23860.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, a researcher attempted to press <i>SCP-23860</i>. The attempt was foiled by security personnel before the button could be pressed. The researcher was immediately terminated and all records related to the incident were erased from all databases.<br><br><h3> Addendum 23860.2: </h3> Interview with Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████, can you tell us about <i>SCP-23860</i>?<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> It's a button that erases history from people's memories and records when pressed. We don't know what it does because we're never going to press it, no matter how tempting it may be. We can't risk the consequences of pressing it, so we're just going to keep it contained and monitored.<br><br><h3> Addendum 23860.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Date:</b> ██/██/████<br><b>Subject:</b> D-23860-1, a Class D personnel with no prior knowledge of <i>SCP-23860</i>.<br><b>Procedure:</b> Subject was instructed to press <i>SCP-23860</i>.<br><b>Result:</b> Upon pressing the button, all memories related to World War II were erased from the collective consciousness of humanity. All records and recollections regarding the war have been completely wiped from existence.</div>
