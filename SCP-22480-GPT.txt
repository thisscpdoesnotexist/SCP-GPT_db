<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-22480 is  Un chat sur un skate</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-22480<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-22480</i> is a domestic shorthair cat (Felis catus) of indeterminate age and gender. It appears to be in good health, though it does not display any signs of aging or physical deterioration.<br><br><i>SCP-22480</i> has the ability to remain perpetually balanced on a skateboard while moving at speeds up to 10 km/h (6 mph). The board itself is made of an unidentified material and does not appear to have any bearings or wheels attached. <i>SCP-22480</i>'s movements are controlled by its tail, which acts as a rudder when steering and provides propulsion by pushing against the ground with each step taken. The skateboard will move in whatever direction <i>SCP-22480</i> wishes, regardless of surface type or incline.<br><br>When not riding its skateboard, <i>SCP-22480</i> will usually sleep curled up on top of it like normal cats do with their beds. It also responds positively to human interaction and can be trained using standard feline training techniques.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-22480</i> is to be kept in a standard containment chamber at Site-██. It should be provided with a skateboard and any other items necessary for its comfort and well-being. Personnel are not allowed to ride the skateboard while <i>SCP-22480</i> is present, as this may cause it distress.<br><br>Any personnel who wish to interact with <i>SCP-22480</i> must do so under the supervision of a Level 2 or higher personnel member.<br><br><h3> Addendum 22480.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-22480</i> was observed to be riding its skateboard in a figure 8 pattern around its containment chamber. Upon further investigation, it was discovered that the board had been modified with a pair of wheels and bearings attached to the bottom.<br><br><h3> Addendum 22480.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-22480</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████████<br><b>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-22480</i><br>&ltBegin Log&gt<br><b>Dr. ████████:</b> Good morning, <i>SCP-22480</i>. How are you today? <br><b><i>SCP-22480</i>:</b> [Meows softly] <br><b>Dr. ████████:</b> Can you tell me why you like to ride the skateboard so much? <br><b><i>SCP-22480</i>:</b> [Purrs and rubs against the skateboard] It's fun! I can go really fast and it's a great way to explore my surroundings without having to walk everywhere. Plus, it's just plain cool! <br><b>Dr. ████████:</b> I see… Do you have any other hobbies or interests? <br><b><i>SCP-22480</i>:</b> [Meows] I like to play with my toys, chase bugs, and sleep in the sun. Oh, and I love cuddling up with my humans! <br>&ltEnd Log&gt<br><b>Closing Statement:</b> <i>SCP-22480</i> appears to be in good health and is content with its current containment arrangements.<br><br><h3> Addendum 22480.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment 22480-1:</b> <i>SCP-22480</i> was placed in a long, straight hallway and instructed to move from one end to the other. It completed the task in approximately 5 minutes, reaching a maximum speed of 8 km/h (5 mph).<br><b><br>Experiment 22480-2:</b> <i>SCP-22480</i> was placed at the top of a staircase and instructed to descend it. It completed the task in approximately 2 minutes, reaching a maximum speed of 10 km/h (6 mph).</div>
