<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-25580 is  a bag of chips that never ends</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-25580<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-25580</i> is an ordinary bag of chips, appearing to be made from a standard variety of potato chips. Upon opening the bag, it is revealed that the contents are infinite in quantity and never deplete no matter how much is taken out. The chips themselves appear to be a generic brand, with no discernible difference from other similar products.<br><br>The origin of <i>SCP-25580</i> is unknown; however, it appears to have been manufactured before its containment by the Foundation. All attempts at tracing its source have failed.<br><br>When consumed, <i>SCP-25580</i> has no anomalous effects on those who eat it aside from providing sustenance as normal food would; however, any person who consumes more than one chip will experience a feeling of intense hunger for more until all the chips in their possession are gone. This effect does not wear off until all remaining chips are consumed or otherwise removed from their vicinity.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-25580</i> is to be contained in a standard containment locker at Site-██. Access to the item is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any personnel who consume more than one chip of <i>SCP-25580</i> are to be monitored for signs of addiction and given psychological counseling as necessary.<br><br><h3> Addendum 25580.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, a Foundation agent infiltrated an underground black market in [REDACTED], where <i>SCP-25580</i> was being sold as a recreational drug. The agent was able to acquire several bags of the chips before being extracted.<br><br><h3> Addendum 25580.2: </h3> Interview with D-25580-1<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Interviewee:</b> D-25580-1, a male subject who was found in possession of <i>SCP-25580</i>.<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Please state your name and age for the record.<br><b><br>D-25580-1:</b> My name is [REDACTED], and I'm 25 years old.<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> How did you come to possess <i>SCP-25580</i>?<br><b><br>D-25580-1:</b> I found it in an alleyway while I was walking home one night. It seemed like a normal bag of chips so I decided to take it with me as a snack for later on that night when I got home.<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Did you experience any unusual effects after consuming <i>SCP-25580</i>?<br><b><br>D-25580-1:</b> Yeah, I started to feel really hungry for more chips even though I had already eaten a few. It was like an uncontrollable craving that wouldn't go away until I ate the whole bag of chips.<br><br><h3> Addendum 25580.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><br>Test A - Two D-Class personnel were given a single chip each and instructed to eat it. Both reported no anomalous effects after consuming the chips.<br><br>Test B - One D-Class personnel was given an entire bag of <i>SCP-25580</i> and instructed to consume as much as they desired. The subject quickly developed an intense craving for more, continuing to eat until all the chips in their possession were gone. Afterward, they reported feeling satisfied but still had a strong desire for more <i>SCP-25580</i>.</div>
