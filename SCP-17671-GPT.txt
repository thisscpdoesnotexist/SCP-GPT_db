<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17671 is  Papa Oni's Pizzeria</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17671<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17671</i> is Papa Oni's Pizzeria, a pizza restaurant located in ████████, Illinois. The interior of the pizzeria appears to be an exact replica of a traditional Italian pizzeria. However, all personnel entering the establishment are immediately affected by a powerful memetic effect which causes them to believe they have been transported back in time to Italy during the early 1900s.<br><br>The memetic effect also causes personnel to become extremely loyal and devoted customers of Papa Oni's Pizzeria. Personnel will remain within the establishment for extended periods of time and will often purchase large amounts of food from the restaurant regardless if they are hungry or not. All personnel that enter <i>SCP-17671</i> experience an intense craving for Italian cuisine and will only consume food purchased from within the establishment even if offered alternatives outside it.<br><br>Additionally, any attempts at leaving <i>SCP-17671</i> before finishing their meal results in extreme distress as well as physical pain that increases with each step taken away from it. Personnel have reported feeling like someone is pulling them back into <i>SCP-17671</i> whenever they try to leave before completing their meal.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17671</i> is to be monitored at all times by two (2) personnel. All personnel entering <i>SCP-17671</i> are to be given Class B amnestics prior to leaving the establishment. Any attempts at leaving <i>SCP-17671</i> before finishing their meal must be prevented and personnel must be escorted out of the establishment by security personnel.<br><br>Any civilians attempting to enter <i>SCP-17671</i> are to be turned away and given a cover story about the restaurant being closed for renovations.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17671.1: </h3> On ██/██/████ an investigation was conducted into the owners of <i>SCP-17671</i>. It was discovered that the restaurant is owned by two brothers, █████ and ████ Oni. The brothers claim to have inherited the pizzeria from their father who had opened it in Italy during the early 1900s. However, no records of such a business exist in any official Italian records and both brothers are unable to provide proof of ownership or even a valid address for the establishment.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17671.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-17671</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-17671</i><br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Hello, <i>SCP-17671</i>. Can you tell me about yourself?<br><b><br><i>SCP-17671</i>:</b> I am Papa Oni's Pizzeria, the best Italian restaurant in town! We serve delicious authentic Italian cuisine and have been in business for over 100 years. Our customers always come back for more because they know that our food is the best around!<br><br><h3> Addendum 17671.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Subject:</b> D-17671-1<br><b><br>Procedure:</b> Subject was instructed to enter <i>SCP-17671</i> and purchase a pizza.<br><b><br>Result:</b> Upon entering <i>SCP-17671</i>, the subject immediately began experiencing the memetic effects of the establishment and remained within it for three (3) hours despite being given multiple opportunities to leave. The subject consumed three (3) large pizzas during this time and reported feeling intense pleasure when eating them. After finishing their meal, the subject appeared to be in an unusually good mood and thanked personnel for allowing them to experience such a wonderful meal before leaving <i>SCP-17671</i>.</div>
