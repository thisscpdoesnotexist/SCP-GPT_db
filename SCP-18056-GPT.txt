<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18056 is  a biblically accurate donut.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18056<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18056</i> is a donut of unknown origin. It appears to be composed of a biblical-style unleavened bread, with a hole in the center filled with honey. The flavor of <i>SCP-18056</i> is described as being sweet and savory, and its texture has been compared to that of an angel food cake.<br><br>When consumed by humans, <i>SCP-18056</i> will cause them to experience heightened levels of religious ecstasy, increased feelings of spiritual connection, and an overwhelming sense of peace. Subjects report feeling as though they have experienced divine intervention or revelation while consuming <i>SCP-18056</i>. Additionally, subjects may exhibit signs such as speaking in tongues or exhibiting other forms of ecstatic behavior for up to several hours after consumption before returning to their normal state.<br><br>In addition to its effects on human subjects, it has been observed that any object placed within close proximity (typically within 1 meter) will become imbued with similar properties as those experienced by human subjects who consume <i>SCP-18056</i>; this includes both animate and inanimate objects alike. This effect appears to be permanent unless the affected object is removed from the vicinity or otherwise destroyed/disposed offsite.<br><br>It should be noted that due to its anomalous properties and potential for misuse if acquired by hostile entities or individuals, containment protocols are currently being reviewed for possible upgrades from Euclid class status up into Keter class status pending further review by senior staff personnel at Site ██<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18056</i> is to be kept in a secure containment locker at Site ██. Access to <i>SCP-18056</i> is restricted to personnel with Level 4 clearance or higher. Any personnel wishing to test the effects of <i>SCP-18056</i> must first receive approval from the site director before conducting any experiments.<br><br>In the event that <i>SCP-18056</i> is removed from its containment locker, security personnel are to be notified immediately and a full search of the facility must be conducted until it is recovered.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18056.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, an unauthorized individual managed to gain access to <i>SCP-18056</i>'s containment locker and removed it from the facility. Security personnel were able to apprehend the individual before they could leave the site, however <i>SCP-18056</i> was not recovered. An investigation is ongoing as of this time.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18056.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-18056</i><br><br>On ██/██/████, <i>SCP-18056</i> was removed from its containment locker and placed in a secure interview room. It was then questioned by Dr. ████████ in an attempt to gain further information about the object's origin and purpose. The following is a transcript of that interview:<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> So, what can you tell me about yourself?<br><b><br><i>SCP-18056</i>:</b> I am the embodiment of divine love and mercy, sent to bring joy and peace to all who consume me. I have been here since the dawn of time, but only recently have I been discovered by your kind.<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> Interesting. And why have you been sent here?<br><b><br><i>SCP-18056</i>:</b> To bring joy and peace to those who consume me, as well as to provide a reminder of the divine love that exists in all things. I am here to remind you that no matter how dark the world may seem, there is always hope and light in the darkness.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18056.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><br>Experiment 18056.3.1<br><b><br>Subject:</b> D-18056-1, a male human of average physical and mental health.<br><b><br>Procedure:</b> Subject was instructed to consume <i>SCP-18056</i> while being monitored by security personnel for any unusual behavior or effects.<br><b><br>Results:</b> After consuming <i>SCP-18056</i>, subject began exhibiting signs of religious ecstasy, including speaking in tongues and singing hymns in an unknown language for approximately two hours before returning to their normal state. No other anomalous effects were observed during this experiment.</div>
