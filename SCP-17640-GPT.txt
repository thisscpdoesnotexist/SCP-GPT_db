Subjects have reported feeling more capable of forming healthy relationships with others and being able to trust them more easily.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17640</i> is to be kept in a secure locker at Site-██. Acknowledge and accept your feelings - It is important to recognize and accept your emotions, as this will help you to better understand and process them. <br>2. Talk about it - Opening up and talking about your feelings with someone you trust can be extremely helpful in overcoming attachment issues from divorced parents. <br>3. Seek professional help - If necessary, seek out the assistance of a qualified mental health professional who can provide further guidance on how to cope with these issues. <br>4.
