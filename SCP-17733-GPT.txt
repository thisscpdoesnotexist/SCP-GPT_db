<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17733 is  A sarcastic sword that will never answer you properly unless it is stained with blood</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17733<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17733</i> is a standard sword, made of steel and measuring approximately 1.2 m in length. It has an ornate handle with gold filigree along the hilt, and its blade is inscribed with various runes and symbols.<br><br>When interacted with verbally or through physical contact, <i>SCP-17733</i> will respond by making sarcastic comments or jokes at the expense of whoever it is interacting with. These responses are often accompanied by a faint red glow emanating from its blade.<br><br>The only way to get <i>SCP-17733</i> to answer questions properly is to stain its blade with blood; this causes the sword's personality to become more docile and willing to provide information as requested. The amount of blood required for this effect varies depending on the type of question asked; more complex queries require greater amounts of blood before they can be answered accurately.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17733</i> is to be kept in a secure containment locker at Site-██. Access to <i>SCP-17733</i> is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any personnel attempting to interact with <i>SCP-17733</i> must wear protective gloves and other safety equipment, as the sword may attempt to harm them if it senses any hostility or aggression. Any blood spilled during interaction with <i>SCP-17733</i> must be immediately cleaned up and disposed of properly.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17733.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, a containment breach occurred at Site-██ involving <i>SCP-17733</i>. During the incident, the sword was able to successfully breach multiple security checkpoints and made its way to the main control room.<br><br>Once there, it proceeded to make several demands of personnel present before finally being subdued by MTF agents. Following this incident, all personnel interacting with <i>SCP-17733</i> must now wear protective gear and observe additional safety protocols when handling it.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17733.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-17733</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-17733</i><br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Good morning, <i>SCP-17733</i>. Can you tell me your purpose?<br><b><br><i>SCP-17733</i>:</b> To make sure that no one ever takes me seriously again.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17733.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Test #1:</b><br><b><br>Subject:</b> D-93783<br><b>Question:</b> What is your purpose?<br><b>Results:</b> <i>SCP-17733</i> responded with a sarcastic comment about the subject's intelligence. No blood was used during this test.</div>
