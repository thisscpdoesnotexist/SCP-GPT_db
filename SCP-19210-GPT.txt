<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-19210 is  Grandma's lutefisk recipe </i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-19210<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Thaumiel<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-19210</i> is a recipe for the traditional Scandinavian dish known as lutefisk. The recipe was written by an unknown elderly woman, who claimed to have been given it by her grandmother.<br><br>The recipe calls for cod, salt, water and lye (sodium hydroxide). When prepared according to the instructions provided in <i>SCP-19210</i>, the resulting dish has several anomalous properties:<br><br>• It can be eaten without any ill effects on the consumer. <br>• It has a flavor that varies depending on who is consuming it; some find it delicious while others find it repulsive. <br>• Its taste and texture are highly addictive; those who consume it often crave more of it even after they have had their fill. <br>• It has healing properties; wounds heal faster when treated with lutefisk prepared from this recipe than with conventional medical treatments. <br>• It appears to be able to suppress certain mental illnesses such as depression and anxiety when consumed regularly over time. <br>• Finally, its effects appear to be cumulative; those who eat larger quantities of lutefisk prepared from this recipe experience greater benefits than those who consume smaller amounts.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-19210</i> is to be stored in a secure locker at Site-██. Access to the recipe is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any attempts to replicate <i>SCP-19210</i> must be approved by the Site Director and monitored closely for any anomalous activity. Any lutefisk prepared from this recipe must be tested for anomalous properties before being consumed, and all testing results must be documented and reported immediately.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19210.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, a lutefisk prepared from <i>SCP-19210</i> was served at a banquet held by the Site Director. All present personnel reported feeling an increased sense of well-being and improved mental clarity following the meal.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19210.2: </h3> Interview with Grandma<br>On ██/██/20██, a Foundation agent posing as a relative of the elderly woman who provided <i>SCP-19210</i> was able to interview her. During the course of the interview, she revealed that she had been given the recipe by her grandmother, who claimed it had been passed down through their family for generations. She also stated that her grandmother had told her not to share it with anyone else, and to keep it secret so that its power could be preserved.<br><br>The elderly woman also revealed that her grandmother had told her that the recipe was a gift from the gods, and that it was meant to be shared with humanity. She said that it could be used to help people in need, and she hoped that one day everyone would be able to benefit from its power.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19210.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment #1:</b> A sample of lutefisk prepared from <i>SCP-19210</i> was given to three volunteers with clinical depression. After one week, all three reported feeling significantly better than before.<br><b><br>Experiment #2:</b> A sample of lutefisk prepared from <i>SCP-19210</i> was given to two volunteers with anxiety disorders. After one week, both reported being much calmer and more relaxed than before.<br><b><br>Conclusion:</b> <i>SCP-19210</i> appears to have a positive effect on mental health when consumed regularly over time. Further testing is recommended in order to determine the full extent of its effects.</div>
