She wears an orange tank top, blue shorts and yellow sneakers similar to those worn by Dora in the show.<br><br><i>SCP-19421</i> is capable of speaking English fluently, though she speaks with a strange accent not heard before on Earth. She claims to be from R'lyeh and states that she was sent back by Cthulhu himself as an ambassador for his people in order to spread knowledge about them throughout the world.<br><br>When asked questions relating to Cthulhu or R'lyeh, <i>SCP-19421</i> will answer them without hesitation or fear; however when asked questions regarding other topics such as history or science she will become confused and unable to answer properly. When questioned about her mission on Earth, <i>SCP-19421</i> will state that it is her duty to teach humanity about Cthulhu's existence and power in order for them prepare themselves for his eventual return from beneath the depths of the sea where he currently resides.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-19421</i> is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. All personnel interacting with <i>SCP-19421</i> must be briefed on the dangers of discussing topics related to Cthulhu or R'lyeh and are not allowed to ask questions about either topic unless authorized by a Level 4 personnel.<br><br>In order to prevent any potential spread of information regarding Cthulhu, all conversations between <i>SCP-19421</i> and personnel must be recorded and reviewed for any potentially dangerous information. Any such information is to be immediately reported to the Site Director for further action.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19421.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, <i>SCP-19421</i> was asked by a researcher if she could say <i>"Ph'nglui mglw'nafh Cthulhu R'lyeh wgah'nagl fhtagn"</i>. She complied without hesitation and proceeded to recite the phrase in its entirety.
