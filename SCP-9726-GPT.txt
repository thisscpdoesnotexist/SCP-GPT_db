This is a very dangerous SCP with lots of victims</i> </h3> </center><br><h3>Item #:</h3> <i>SCP-9726</i><br><br><h3>Object Class:</h3> Safe<br><br><h3>Special Containment Procedures:</h3> <i>SCP-9726</i> is to be kept in a locked box at Site ██.<br><br><h3>Description:</h3> <i>SCP-9726</i> is a compass with the words <i>"Gaga's fan"</i> engraved on it. The needle of <i>SCP-9726</i> always points to the nearest fan of Lady Gaga, even if they are in a different country.<br><br><i>SCP-9726</i> was discovered by Agent ██████ who found it while he was traveling in ███████, Canada. He took it with him and brought it back to Site ██ for testing and storage.<br><br><h3>Addendum 9726.1 : </h3>**********<br><br>Test Log 9726-1:<br><br><b>Subject: </b>D-9726 (Female, age 25)<br><br><b>Procedure: </b>Subject was told to walk around the testing room with <i>SCP-9726</i>. The needle of <i>SCP-9726</i> will point to the nearest fan of Lady Gaga.<br><br><b>Results: </b>The needle pointed straight to Subject's chest. When questioned about it, she said that she is a big fan of Lady Gaga and that she listens to her music every day. She also said that she has all her albums and merchandise and even went as far as saying that <i>"Lady Gaga is my life"</i>. After this statement, the needle turned 180 degrees from where it was pointing before.<br><br><b>Notes: </b>It seems like <i>SCP-9726</i> works on people who are fans of Lady Gaga by default but if they say something positive about her or show some kind of affection towards her, it will change its direction towards them immediately.
