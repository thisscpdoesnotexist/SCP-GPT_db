<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-24336 is  a medusa but with geese instead of snakes</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-24336<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-24336</i> is an anomalous humanoid entity, resembling a female human in her 30s. The subject's head is replaced with that of a large goose, which appears to be anatomically correct and capable of normal avian movement. <i>SCP-24336</i>'s body is covered in white feathers and its wings are fully functional.<br><br>The entity has displayed sapience, speaking English fluently and demonstrating the ability to reason logically. It has also demonstrated knowledge of various occult topics such as alchemy and astrology, although it claims not to have any direct experience with them. When asked about its origin or purpose, <i>SCP-24336</i> simply states that it <i>"exists"</i>.<br><br><i>SCP-24336</i> displays no hostility towards personnel but exhibits extreme caution when approached by humans; if confronted directly it will attempt to flee or hide until the threat passes. It will only make contact with personnel if offered food or shelter; otherwise it remains at a distance from humans while observing their activities intently. While under observation, <i>SCP-24336</i> shows signs of distress when exposed to loud noises or bright lights; these can cause the entity to become agitated and unpredictable in behavior for up to several hours afterwards<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-24336</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. The cell should be equipped with a secure door and windows, as well as appropriate lighting and soundproofing.<br><br>Personnel assigned to interact with <i>SCP-24336</i> must wear protective clothing and remain at least 3 meters away from the entity at all times. All food items offered to <i>SCP-24336</i> must first be inspected for any potential contaminants or hazardous substances; if found, they are not to be given to the entity.<br><br>Any attempts by personnel to approach or capture <i>SCP-24336</i> are strictly prohibited without prior authorization from a Level 4 personnel or higher. In the event of an escape attempt, MTF units are authorized to use nonlethal<br><br><h3> Addendum 24336.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, <i>SCP-24336</i> was observed to be in possession of a small metallic object resembling a pocket watch. Upon closer inspection, the object was found to contain a complex mechanism and an inscription written in an unknown language. Research into the object is ongoing.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24336.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-24336</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████<br><b><br>Interviewee:</b> <i>SCP-24336</i><br><br><b>[BEGIN LOG]<br><br>Dr. ████:</b> Good morning, <i>SCP-24336</i>. Can you tell me your name?<br><b><br><i>SCP-24336</i>:</b> I have no name. I exist, that is all.<br><b><br>Dr. ████:</b> What can you tell me about yourself?<br><b><br><i>SCP-24336</i>:</b> I am a creature of the night, born from the darkness and chaos of the world. I have no purpose or goal, only to exist in this form until my time is up.<br><b><br>Dr. ████:</b> Do you have any memories of your past?<br><b><br><i>SCP-24336</i>:</b> I remember nothing before my current form. I do not know how or why I came to be this way. All I know is that this is what I am now, and must accept it as such.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24336.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log 24336.1:</b> On ██/██/20██, a standard containment cell was set up for <i>SCP-24336</i> at Site-██. The entity was offered various food items in an attempt to gauge its diet preferences; it accepted all items without hesitation and appeared to enjoy them immensely.<br><b><br>Experiment Log 24336.2:</b> On ██/██/20██, personnel attempted to communicate with <i>SCP-24336</i> using basic sign language; the entity responded by displaying signs of confusion and distress before fleeing from the chamber. It has since refused any further attempts at communication from personnel.</div>
