<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-24125 is  the telltale fart</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-24125<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-24125</i> is an anomalous phenomenon which manifests as a noxious odor, resembling that of flatulence. This odor is capable of traveling through walls and other solid objects, and can be detected from up to 5 kilometers away.<br><br>When the scent of <i>SCP-24125</i> is inhaled by a human subject, they will experience an extreme sense of dread and fear. Subjects will often become disoriented and confused, leading to panic attacks or even complete mental breakdowns in some cases. In addition, subjects may become aggressive towards those around them due to their heightened emotional state.<br><br>The source of <i>SCP-24125</i> remains unknown; however, it appears to originate from somewhere within the United States.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> As <i>SCP-24125</i> is an airborne phenomenon, containment is impossible. However, personnel are to be dispatched to investigate any reports of the odor and attempt to trace its source.<br><br>In addition, all personnel exposed to <i>SCP-24125</i> must be monitored for signs of mental distress or aggression. If necessary, amnestics may be administered in order to mitigate any psychological effects caused by exposure.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24125.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, a large number of reports of the odor were received from various locations around the United States. Upon investigation, it was determined that all of these reports originated from a single source in [REDACTED].<br><br>Personnel dispatched to investigate found an individual matching the description given by witnesses. The individual was carrying a backpack containing several bottles filled with an unknown liquid. When questioned, they refused to answer any questions and ran away before personnel could detain them.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24125.2: </h3> Interview with Subject ██<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████████<br><b>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-24125</i> Test Subject ██<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> Can you tell me what happened when you encountered the smell?<br><b><br>Subject ██:</b> It was like I was suddenly filled with terror, like something terrible was about to happen. I couldn't think straight and my heart started racing. Then I just felt this overwhelming urge to run away as fast as possible.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24125.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log 24125.3-1:</b> On ██/██/20██, an experiment was conducted to test the effects of <i>SCP-24125</i> on human subjects. Five D-Class personnel were exposed to a sample of the odor in a sealed chamber and monitored for any changes in behavior or mental state.<br><b><br>Results:</b> All five subjects experienced extreme fear and panic upon exposure to <i>SCP-24125</i>, leading to increased heart rate, sweating, and difficulty breathing. Three of the subjects also exhibited signs of aggression towards each other before being sedated by security personnel. No long term psychological damage was reported after administering amnestics to all five subjects.</div>
