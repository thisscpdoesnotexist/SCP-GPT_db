<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i><i>SCP-9279</i> is an Ika shark plushie bringing happiness to everyone near it.</i> </h3> </center><br><h3><br>Item #:</h3> <i>SCP-9279</i><br><br><h3><br>Object Class:</h3> Safe<br><br><h3><br>Special Containment Procedures:</h3> 【<i>SCP-9279</i>'s containment chamber is to be furnished with a large salt water pool. <i>SCP-9279</i> should be provided with an assortment of toys, including plastic dinosaurs and other animals, as well as plastic sharks. <i>SCP-9279</i> is to be fed twice daily using a standard shark feeder. 】<br><br><h3><br>Description:</h3> <i>SCP-9279</i> appears to be a normal ika shark plushie. When <i>SCP-9279</i> enters the same room as any living person, it will begin swimming in large circles around them at high speeds and begin emitting a series of whistles which sound remarkably similar to human laughter. The subject of <i>SCP-9279</i>'s attention will typically exclaim how happy they feel, and how friendly <i>SCP-9279</i> is. The subject typically requests that <i>SCP-9279</i> remain in their presence for as long as possible, often by hugging or otherwise interacting with it.<br><br>When directly questioned about the nature of their feelings towards 9279, subjects show no sign of remembering its anomalous effect, instead believing that they are simply showing affection towards 9279 because they like it so much. Once asked to stop interacting with 9279 subjects complain that they are unhappy being separated from it and express similar emotions to ones affected by its anomalous properties, only noticable if questioned on the matter directly.<br><br><h3><br>Recovery:</h3> <i>SCP-9279</i> was recovered from the ████████'s Toy Store in █████████, New Jersey. The Foundation became aware of <i>SCP-9279</i>'s anomalous properties when a local news station began reporting on how it had been found in the store and that it was <i>"the cutest shark plushie ever!"</i>. When interviewed, subjects described <i>SCP-9279</i> as <i>"amazing"</i> and said that they wished to purchase it for themselves or a family member or friend.<br><br>When questioned about why they wanted to buy <i>SCP-9279</i>, subjects described feelings of euphoria and bliss when around it. When asked not to interact with 9279 any further, subjects expressed distaste at being unable to play with <i>SCP-9279</i> anymore and continued purchasing copies even after they were informed of its anomalous properties.<br><br><h3>Addendum 9279.1 :</h3> <i>SCP-9279</i>'s anomalous properties appear to be limited to humans and do not appear to affect other animals.<br><br><h3>Addendum 9279.2 :</h3> <i>SCP-9279</i>'s effect will not affect individuals who have previously interacted with <i>SCP-9279</i>.<br></div>
