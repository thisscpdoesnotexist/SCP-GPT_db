<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i><i>SCP-9720</i> is a yellow mouse causing deadly electric perturbations. <i>SCP-9712</i> is really cute</i> </h3> </center><br><h3>Item #:</h3> <i>SCP-9720</i><br><br><h3>Object Class:</h3> Euclid<br><br><h3>Special Containment Procedures:</h3> <i>SCP-9720</i> is to be kept in a small cage made of metal, with a layer of rubber on the bottom. It is to be fed once a day with any kind of mouse food.<br><br><h3>Description:</h3> <i>SCP-9720</i> is a yellow mouse of unknown origin. It's anomalous properties are only activated when it comes in contact with other mice, or a human being.<br><br><i>SCP-9720</i> will emit an electric shock that will kill the mouse/human that came in contact with it, and any other mice within 5 meters of the subject. The shock can be blocked by metal objects or rubber gloves, but not by plastic or wood.<br><br><i>SCP-9720</i> was found in ████████, England after reports of several people dying from an electric shock were reported to local authorities. After SCP agents investigated the area where the deaths occurred they found <i>SCP-9720</i> hiding behind a trashcan near one victim's house. After retrieving it for testing and classifying it as SCP 9720, all victims were buried because their bodies were too damaged to be kept for study .</div>
