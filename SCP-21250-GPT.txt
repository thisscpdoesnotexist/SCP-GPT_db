It stands approximately 1 meter tall and has a wide grin painted on its face. Its limbs are jointed in such a way as to allow it to move freely.<br><br>When inactive, <i>SCP-21250</i> will remain motionless and silent. When active, it will sing cheerful songs or tell jokes with an enthusiastic tone of voice. It is capable of speaking in multiple languages and appears to be able to understand most spoken languages as well; however it does not respond directly when addressed by name or other identifiers.<br><br><i>SCP-21250</i>'s anomalous properties manifest when any living creature comes within 10 meters of the object while singing or telling jokes; this causes all persons within range to become overwhelmed with joy and laughter for the duration of the performance (typically lasting 5 minutes). Afterward they will experience extreme fatigue for up to 48 hours before returning back to normal behavior patterns. Persons affected by this phenomenon have described feeling <i>"happier than ever before"</i>.<br><br>The effects of <i>SCP-21250</i>'s performance can be negated if those affected are removed from its presence during the song/joke session; however if left exposed for more than 15 seconds after commencement then they must complete the full duration before showing any signs of recovery.
