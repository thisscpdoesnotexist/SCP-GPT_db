Testing with <i>SCP-10597</i> must take place in an outdoor area surrounded by a fence tall enough that <i>SCP-10597</i> cannot fly over it, and must only take place during daylight hours.<br><br><h3>Description:</h3> <i>SCP-10597</i> is a flock of birds (species unconfirmed, but presumed to be European Starlings) that are capable of mimicking human speech. <i>SCP-10597</i> is not sapient, and will only mimic human speech.<br><br><i>SCP-10597</i> was discovered in ████████, Utah by Agent ██████ on August 12th, 20██. After this interview, <i>SCP-10597</i> began mimicking the voices of several other Foundation personnel that it had heard over the speaker system during the interview with Dr. ██████.<br><br><h3>Addendum 10597.3 : </h3>Experiment Log <br><br>+Experiment 10597-1 -Experiment 10597-1 <br><br><b>Test: </b>Dr. ██████ attempts to interview <i>SCP-10597</i> through a speaker system. <br><br><b>Results: </b><i>SCP-10597</i> did not respond to Dr. ██████'s questions, but began making statements such as <i>"I can't sleep"</i> and <i>"I'm so lonely"</i>.<br><br>+Experiment 10597-2 -Experiment 10597-2 <br><br><b>Test: </b>A live chicken is placed in the aviary with <i>SCP-10597</i> and observed for three days. <br><br><b>Results: </b>During the first day, <i>SCP-10597</i> did not interact with the chicken at all, but on the second day it began pecking at it and attempting to eat it .
