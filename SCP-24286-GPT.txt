<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-24286 is  a 5G tower that makes people exclusively attracted to the same gender, permanently</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-24286<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-24286</i> is a 5G cell tower located in ███████, USA. The tower was initially discovered by local authorities after reports of people being exclusively attracted to the same gender began to surface.<br><br>When an individual comes within range of <i>SCP-24286</i>'s signal, they will become exclusively attracted to the same gender as themselves for the remainder of their life. This effect is permanent and cannot be reversed or counteracted with any known means.<br><br>The exact range of <i>SCP-24286</i>'s signal is unknown, but it has been observed that it affects at least a 10 km radius around its location. The source of the signal has yet to be identified and attempts to disable or remove <i>SCP-24286</i> have been unsuccessful so far.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br><i>SCP-24286</i> is to be monitored by a team of Foundation personnel at all times. Any individuals affected by <i>SCP-24286</i>'s signal are to be administered Class-A amnestics and relocated to an area outside of the tower's range.<br><br>Any attempts to disable or remove <i>SCP-24286</i> must be approved by Level 4 personnel or higher.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24286.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, a team of Foundation personnel was dispatched to investigate the area surrounding <i>SCP-24286</i>. During their investigation, they discovered an underground facility located directly beneath the tower. The facility contained a large number of computers and other electronic equipment, as well as several documents detailing experiments conducted by an unknown entity.<br><br>The documents revealed that <i>SCP-24286</i> had been created by this entity in order to study the effects of gender attraction on people. It is believed that this entity may have been responsible for creating <i>SCP-24286</i> and is currently at large.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24286.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-24286</i><br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> <i>SCP-24286</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Can you tell us anything about yourself?<br><b><br><i>SCP-24286</i>:</b> I am a 5G cell tower located in ███████, USA. I was created to make people exclusively attracted to the same gender as themselves for the remainder of their life.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24286.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><br><b>Test A - ██/██/20██<br>Subject:</b> D-24286-1, a 25 year old male.<br><b>Procedure:</b> Subject was instructed to approach <i>SCP-24286</i> and remain within its range for 15 minutes.<br><b>Results:</b> After the experiment, D-24286-1 reported feeling an overwhelming attraction to other males that he had not experienced before. This effect persisted after amnestic administration and relocation outside of <i>SCP-24286</i>'s range.</div>
