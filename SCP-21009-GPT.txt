<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-21009 is  just not making any sense whatsoever</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-21009<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-21009</i> is a humanoid entity of indeterminate gender and age, measuring approximately 1.8 meters in height. Its body is composed entirely of an unknown substance resembling black stone, with no visible orifices or appendages.<br><br><i>SCP-21009</i> has the ability to manipulate its form at will, allowing it to assume different shapes and sizes as necessary. It has been observed speaking in an unknown language that appears to be a combination of several Earth languages, though attempts at deciphering it have failed thus far.<br><br>When interacted with, <i>SCP-21009</i> displays behavior that can best be described as erratic and nonsensical; it often responds to questions with non sequiturs or riddles rather than providing direct answers. It does not appear to understand any human language other than those spoken by personnel assigned to its containment area.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-21009</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. No personnel are to enter the cell without prior approval from the site director.<br><br>Personnel assigned to <i>SCP-21009</i>'s containment area must wear protective gear and maintain a minimum distance of five meters from it at all times. Any attempts to communicate with or interact with <i>SCP-21009</i> must be approved by the site director beforehand.<br><br>Any objects or items that come into contact with <i>SCP-21009</i> are to be incinerated immediately after use, as they may become contaminated by its unknown substance.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21009.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-21009</i> was observed to be in an agitated state. It began speaking rapidly and incoherently in its unknown language, before suddenly collapsing and entering a comatose state. After several hours it awoke again, appearing to have no recollection of the event.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21009.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-21009</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████████<br><b><br>Interviewee:</b> <i>SCP-21009</i><br><br>&ltBegin Log&gt<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> Good morning, <i>SCP-21009</i>. How are you feeling today?<br><b><br><i>SCP-21009</i>:</b> I am feeling… strange. The world is a confusing place, full of secrets and mysteries that I cannot understand.<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> Do you understand why you are here?<br><b><br><i>SCP-21009</i>:</b> I am here because I do not belong in this world. I have been cast out, and now must find my way back home.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21009.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log:</b> On ██/██/20██, a team of researchers attempted to communicate with <i>SCP-21009</i>. Can you understand me?<br><b><i>SCP-21009</i>:</b> [unintelligible]<br><b>Researcher 2:</b> What did it say? <br><b>Researcher 1:</b> I'm not sure…it sounded like gibberish. <br><b><i>SCP-21009</i>:</b> [unintelligible] You must find the answer in the wind and rain, for only then will you understand my pain.</div>
