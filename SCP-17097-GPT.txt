<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17097 is  a tricky lobster man who likes to steal.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17097<h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17097</i> is a humanoid entity approximately 1.5 meters in height and weighing approximately 80 kilograms. It has an orange-brown exoskeleton, two large claws on its hands, and four black eyes.<br><br><i>SCP-17097</i>'s primary anomalous property is that it can manipulate objects within a certain radius of itself with its mind, allowing it to steal items from nearby locations without physically touching them. <i>SCP-17097</i> also possesses enhanced strength and agility, allowing it to move quickly and climb surfaces with ease.<br><br>When not actively stealing objects or attempting to escape containment, <i>SCP-17097</i> will often attempt to interact with personnel by asking for food or other items that it desires. If denied these requests, <i>SCP-17097</i> may become hostile towards personnel or attempt to find ways of obtaining the desired item itself using its anomalous abilities.<br><br><i>SCP-17097</i> must be contained in a sealed container at all times due to the risk posed by its ability to steal items without physical contact; any attempts at escape must be dealt with swiftly and decisively by security personnel assigned to guard the object's containment chamber.<h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17097</i> is to be contained in a sealed, reinforced steel chamber at all times. The chamber must be monitored by security personnel at all times and any attempts at escape must be dealt with swiftly and decisively.<br><br>Personnel interacting with <i>SCP-17097</i> are to remain outside of the containment chamber and communicate through an intercom system. All requests for items made by <i>SCP-17097</i> are to be denied immediately; if it becomes hostile or attempts to escape, appropriate force is authorized for containment purposes.<h3> Addendum 17097.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, <i>SCP-17097</i> was observed attempting to escape containment by using its anomalous abilities to manipulate a nearby security guard's gun. The incident was quickly contained and the security guard in question has been reassigned.<h3> Addendum 17097.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-17097</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-17097</i><br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Hello, <i>SCP-17097</i>. Can you tell me why you steal things?<br><b><br><i>SCP-17097</i>:</b> I don't know… I just like it. It's fun and makes me feel powerful.<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Do you understand how dangerous your actions are?<br><b><br><i>SCP-17097</i>:</b> Yes, I do. But it's too hard to resist the temptation.<h3> Addendum 17097.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log 17097.3-A:</b> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-17097</i> was presented with a variety of items in its containment chamber and instructed to take them using its anomalous abilities. The following items were successfully taken by <i>SCP-17097</i>:<br><br>• One (1) apple<br>• Two (2) pencils<br>• Three (3) pieces of candy<br><b>• Four (4) coins Results:</b> <i>SCP-17097</i> was able to successfully take all four items without physically touching them. It then proceeded to consume the apple and candy before returning the other two objects to their original locations.</div>
