<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18271 is  a legendary pair of knee-high stockings that once belonged to a demon</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18271<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18271</i> is a pair of knee-high stockings made of an unknown material. The stockings are black in color and have a faint, glowing red pattern around the cuffs.<br><br>When worn by any human being, <i>SCP-18271</i> will cause them to become possessed by an entity believed to be a demon from another plane of existence. This entity appears to possess knowledge and abilities far beyond those of ordinary humans, as well as the ability to manipulate physical reality on a limited scale. It has been observed that subjects under the influence of <i>SCP-18271</i> exhibit increased strength and speed, along with enhanced senses and reflexes.<br><br>The only known way to remove <i>SCP-18271</i>'s effects is for its wearer to voluntarily take it off or be forcibly removed from it by another individual or object. Once removed, all anomalous effects cease immediately and no further possession attempts have been observed since then.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18271</i> is to be kept in a standard containment locker at Site-██. Access to <i>SCP-18271</i> must be approved by two Level 4 personnel or higher.<br><br>In the event of a containment breach, all personnel are to immediately evacuate the area and contact MTF Gamma-5 (<i>"Red Herrings"</i>) for assistance.<br><br>Any personnel found wearing <i>SCP-18271</i> are to be detained and questioned regarding their possession experience before being released into Foundation custody.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18271.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, a containment breach occurred at Site-██. During the incident, <i>SCP-18271</i> was stolen by an unknown individual. All attempts to locate and retrieve it have been unsuccessful.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18271.2: </h3> Interview with Subject<br><br>On ██/██/20██, a subject wearing <i>SCP-18271</i> was found in the city of [REDACTED] and taken into Foundation custody. The following is an excerpt from their interview:<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> How did you come across <i>SCP-18271</i>?<br><b><br>Subject:</b> I…I don't really know. I found it in my closet one day and decided to try them on. They felt so…right, like they were meant for me or something. It's like they had a power that drew me to them. And then when I put them on, everything changed. It was like I could feel this presence inside me, guiding my actions and thoughts. It was almost comforting at first, but then it started to become overwhelming.<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Do you know what this entity is?<br><b><br>Subject:</b> No, I don't. All I know is that it's powerful and doesn't seem to have any interest in leaving me alone.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18271.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Date:</b> ██/██/20██<br><b>Subject:</b> D-18271-1<br><b>Procedure:</b> <i>SCP-18271</i> was placed on D-18271-1's legs.<br><b>Results:</b> Upon donning <i>SCP-18271</i>, D-18271-1 began to exhibit signs of possession. The subject displayed enhanced strength and speed, as well as increased reflexes and senses. After approximately 15 minutes of observation, the subject suddenly removed <i>SCP-18271</i> from their legs and returned to normal behavior.</div>
