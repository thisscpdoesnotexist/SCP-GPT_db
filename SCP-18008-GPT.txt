<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18008 is  Lloyd in Space</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18008<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18008</i> is a humanoid entity resembling the character Lloyd Nebulon from the animated television show Lloyd in Space. <i>SCP-18008</i> appears to be an exact physical copy of the character, though its behavior does not match that of its source material.<br><br><i>SCP-18008</i> has displayed various anomalous abilities, including flight, telekinesis and teleportation. It is capable of communicating with humans through speech, though it typically speaks in a robotic monotone voice. <i>SCP-18008</i> also displays advanced knowledge of technology and engineering principles far beyond what would be expected for a fictional character such as itself.<br><br>When questioned about its origin or purpose, <i>SCP-18008</i> will often respond with vague statements such as <i>"I am here to help"</i> or <i>"My mission is to protect"</i>. It has been observed attempting to assist personnel on several occasions without being asked to do so. However, attempts to communicate further have thus far been unsuccessful due to <i>SCP-18008</i>'s limited understanding of human language and culture.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18008</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. It is to be monitored by personnel at all times and provided with basic amenities such as food, water, and entertainment.<br><br>Any attempts to communicate with <i>SCP-18008</i> must be approved by the current Site Director or higher authority. Any requests for assistance from <i>SCP-18008</i> should also be approved before being granted.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18008.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-18008</i> was observed attempting to construct a spacecraft.
