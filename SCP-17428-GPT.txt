<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17428 is  a red cape that sets your Wanted level to max.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17428<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17428</i> is a red cape made of an unknown material. When worn by any human subject, their Wanted level in any game or virtual environment will be set to the maximum value. This effect does not appear to have any effect on non-human entities.<br><br>Once <i>SCP-17428</i> has been removed from the wearer, their Wanted level will reset to its original value. However, if <i>SCP-17428</i> is reworn by the same individual within 24 hours of being removed, they will again receive a max Wanted level.<br><br>The origin and creator of <i>SCP-17428</i> remain unknown at this time.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17428</i> is to be kept in a secure locker at Site-██. Access to <i>SCP-17428</i> is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any testing involving <i>SCP-17428</i> must be approved by the Site Director and conducted under the supervision of at least two (2) security personnel.
