The entity's skin is composed of a soft, rubbery material similar to latex rubber, which appears to be impervious to damage.<br><br><i>SCP-20625</i> displays many similarities with <i>SCP-173</i>; however, instead of being immobile and hostile towards human beings, it is capable of movement and highly sociable. It will often attempt to interact with personnel on sight by speaking in an engaging manner or attempting physical contact such as hugging or caressing them.<br><br>The entity's anomalous properties become apparent when it attempts physical contact with any living organism other than itself; upon making contact, the subject will enter a state of suspended animation for up to twelve hours before returning to normal consciousness without any memory of the incident taking place. During this time period, <i>SCP-20625</i> will remain completely still and unresponsive until the effect wears off.<br><br>Any attempts at restraining or containing <i>SCP-20625</i> have been unsuccessful due its ability to move freely through solid objects (including walls). As such containment procedures are currently limited to observation only.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-20625</i> is to be kept in a standard humanoid containment chamber at all times.
