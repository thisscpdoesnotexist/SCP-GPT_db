It is approximately 1.7 meters tall and wears a black leather trench coat, fedora hat, dark sunglasses, and a gas mask that obscures its face. Its body appears to be composed of an unknown material resembling black rubber or plastic.<br><br><i>SCP-22026</i> has been observed to possess enhanced strength and agility beyond what would be expected from any known human being. It also possesses the ability to manipulate electromagnetic fields in order to produce various effects such as levitation or creating force fields around itself for protection from harm.<br><br><i>SCP-22026</i>'s primary purpose appears to be tracking down criminals who have committed crimes against the future society it inhabits. Whenever it encounters such individuals, it will attempt to apprehend them using whatever means necessary including physical force if needed. Once apprehended they are taken away by <i>SCP-22026</i> for unknown purposes though they are never seen again afterwards.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br><i>SCP-22026</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. Access to the containment area must only be granted with Level 4 clearance or higher.<br><br>In order to prevent <i>SCP-22026</i> from escaping, it should not be allowed access to any form of technology or communication devices. Any attempts by <i>SCP-22026</i> to breach its containment must be reported immediately and dealt with swiftly and appropriately.<br><br>Any personnel who come into contact with <i>SCP-22026</i> are advised not to engage it in conversation as this may provoke an aggressive response from the entity.
