<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-20007 is  A story in a post-apocalyptic world</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-20007<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-20007</i> is a post-apocalyptic story written in the form of a novel. The novel follows the adventures of two survivors, John and Jane, as they traverse through a world devastated by an unknown cataclysmic event.<br><br>The narrative begins with John and Jane waking up in an abandoned bunker with no memory of how they got there or what happened to the world outside. As they explore their surroundings, they soon discover that the surface is now uninhabitable due to extreme radiation levels. With no other option, they must brave the dangerous underground tunnels in search of food and supplies while trying to stay one step ahead from various hostile creatures that inhabit them. Along their journey, John and Jane uncover clues that hint at what caused this disaster as well as hints about their own pasts which may be connected to it all. <br><br>Throughout the story, themes such as hope in times of despair, courage against overwhelming odds and friendship even when all else fails are explored through vivid descriptions and engaging dialogue between characters. <br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-20007</i> is to be kept in a secure locker at Site-██. Access to the novel is restricted and only personnel with Level 3 clearance or above are allowed to read it.<br><br>Any personnel who have read <i>SCP-20007</i> must undergo psychological evaluation before being cleared for further duty.<br><br><h3> Addendum 20007.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, a written request was made by Dr. █████ to allow personnel with Level 2 clearance or above to read <i>SCP-20007</i>. The request was denied due to the potentially hazardous psychological effects of reading the novel.<br><br><h3> Addendum 20007.2: </h3> Interview with Author<br><br>On ██/██/████, an interview was conducted with the author of <i>SCP-20007</i>. The following is a transcript of the interview: <br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Can you tell us more about your story? <br><b><br>Author:</b> Sure. It's set in a post-apocalyptic world where most of humanity has been wiped out by some unknown cataclysmic event. John and Jane are two survivors who must brave the dangers of this new world in order to find food and supplies while trying to uncover clues about their pasts that may be connected to what happened. Along the way, they also discover themes such as hope in times of despair, courage against overwhelming odds and friendship even when all else fails. <br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> What inspired you to write this story? <br><b><br>Author:</b> I wanted to explore the idea of how people can come together and find hope even in the darkest of times. I also wanted to show that no matter what happens, there is always something worth fighting for.<br><br><h3> Addendum 20007.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><br>On ██/██/████, a test was conducted to measure the effects of <i>SCP-20007</i> on human subjects. Five personnel were selected and instructed to read the novel in its entirety. Upon completion, all five reported feeling a sense of hope and optimism despite the bleak setting described in the story. It is theorized that this effect may be caused by <i>SCP-20007</i>'s ability to evoke strong emotions from readers. Further testing is required to confirm this hypothesis.</div>
