<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17958 is  a marijuana dryad</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17958<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17958</i> is an anomalous humanoid entity resembling a female human with green skin and foliage in place of hair. <i>SCP-17958</i> is capable of manipulating the growth, potency, and flavor of any marijuana plant within a 50 meter radius.<br><br><i>SCP-17958</i> appears to be sapient but has yet to communicate verbally or otherwise. It is believed that <i>SCP-17958</i> originated from the cannabis sativa plant species native to Central America.<br><br>When agitated, <i>SCP-17958</i> will emit a pungent smell similar to burning marijuana leaves. Any attempts at containment have been unsuccessful due to its ability to manipulate plants for escape purposes.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17958</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. The cell must have a minimum of three (3) meters of soil and vegetation for <i>SCP-17958</i> to manipulate.<br><br>Personnel are not allowed to enter the containment chamber without authorization from Level 3 personnel or higher. Any attempts at communication with <i>SCP-17958</i> must be approved by the same personnel prior to implementation.<br><br>In order to prevent any potential escape attempts, all entrances and exits within fifty (50) meters of <i>SCP-17958</i>'s containment chamber must be monitored by security cameras at all times.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17958.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, a group of researchers attempted to communicate with <i>SCP-17958</i>. The following conversation ensued:<br><b><br>Researcher 1:</b> <i>"Hello? Can you hear me?"</i><br><b><br><i>SCP-17958</i>:</b> [No response]<br><b><br>Researcher 2:</b> <i>"We mean you no harm. We just want to talk."</i> <br><b><br><i>SCP-17958</i>:</b> [No response] <br><b>  Researcher 3:</b> <i>"Do you understand us?"</i> <br><b><br> <i>SCP-17958</i>:</b> [Silence for approximately five (5) seconds before emitting a pungent smell similar to burning marijuana leaves.]<br><br><h3> Addendum 17958.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-17958</i><br><br>On ██/██/████, personnel were able to successfully communicate with <i>SCP-17958</i>. The following is a transcript of the interview.<br><br>&ltBegin Log&gt<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Can you tell us your name?<br><b><br><i>SCP-17958</i>:</b> You may call me Maria.<br><b><br> Interviewer:</b> How did you come to be here?<br><b><br> <i>SCP-17958</i>:</b> I was born from the earth and its plants. I am a part of this world, and it is my duty to protect it. <br><b><br> Interviewer:</b> What do you mean by that? <br><b><br> <i>SCP-17958</i>:</b> I use my power to ensure that marijuana plants are grown in abundance and remain safe from harm. It is my mission to keep them safe and healthy. <br><b><br> Interviewer:</b> What do you think of humans? <br><b><br> <i>SCP-17958</i>:</b> Humans are destructive creatures, but they can also be kind and compassionate. I believe that if they use their power for good, then the world can become a better place. <br><b><br> Interviewer:</b> Do you have any other thoughts or comments? <br><b><br> <i>SCP-17958</i>:</b> Yes. I wish for humans to respect the power of nature and to use it wisely.<br><br>&ltEnd Log&gt<br><b><br>Closing Statement:</b> <i>SCP-17958</i> appears to be a benevolent entity with an interest in protecting marijuana plants. It is currently unknown if it has any other objectives.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17958.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log 1:</b><br><b><br>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-17958</i><br><b><br>Procedure:</b> A marijuana plant was placed in the containment chamber with <i>SCP-17958</i>. The plant was monitored for any changes in growth, potency, or flavor.<br><b><br>Results:</b> After a few minutes of observation, it became apparent that the marijuana plant had grown significantly larger and more potent than before. Its leaves also changed color to a deep green hue and its smell became much more pungent.</div>
