<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18166 is  a chaos dragon that protects the planet Alpha from</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18166<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18166</i> is a large, dragon-like creature of indeterminate species, approximately 30 meters in length. It has four wings, two short horns on its head and an elongated tail. Its scales are composed of an unknown material that appears to be constantly shifting between various colors and patterns.<br><br><i>SCP-18166</i> was discovered in the Alpha system orbiting a small planetoid designated as <i>SCP-18166</i>-1. The planetoid is believed to have been created by <i>SCP-18166</i> itself for the purpose of protecting it from outside interference or harm.<br><br><i>SCP-18166</i> does not appear to require sustenance or sleep; instead, it spends most of its time patrolling the area around <i>SCP-18166</i>-1 and responding to any perceived threats with extreme aggression. If provoked or attacked, SCP 18166 will employ powerful magical attacks that can cause damage over long distances. It has also demonstrated the ability to teleport at will throughout the Alpha system without requiring a physical form of transportation.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18166</i> is to be monitored by Foundation personnel stationed at <i>SCP-18166</i>-1. Any attempts to approach or interfere with <i>SCP-18166</i> are prohibited without prior authorization from the Site Director.<br><br>In the event of a containment breach, MTF Beta-7 (<i>"Dragon Slayers"</i>) is to be dispatched to reestablish containment of SCP 18166.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18166.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, a Foundation exploration team was dispatched to investigate <i>SCP-18166</i>-1. Upon arrival, the team encountered <i>SCP-18166</i> and attempted to communicate with it. After several attempts at communication, SCP 18166 ceased its aggressive behavior and began speaking in an unknown language.<br><br>The following is a transcript of the conversation between the exploration team and SCP 18166:<br><b><br>Exploration Team:</b> <i>"Hello. We are here on behalf of the Foundation."</i> <br><b>SCP 18166:</b> <i>"I am Alpha's guardian. I protect this world from all who would do it harm."</i> <br><b>Exploration Team:</b> <i>"What can you tell us about your purpose?"</i> <br><b>SCP 18166:</b> <i>"My purpose is simple; I exist to protect Alpha from those who wish to exploit or destroy it."</i><br><br><h3> Addendum 18166.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-18166</i><br><br>On ██/██/20██, a Foundation personnel was able to establish communication with <i>SCP-18166</i>. During the interview, <i>SCP-18166</i> revealed that it had been protecting the Alpha system for centuries and that it considered itself to be its guardian. It also claimed to have created <i>SCP-18166</i>-1 in order to ensure its own safety as well as the safety of those who inhabit the Alpha system.<br><br>When asked why it had chosen to protect the Alpha system, <i>SCP-18166</i> stated that it was driven by a sense of duty and responsibility. It claimed that its primary goal was to ensure that no harm would come to those who lived in the Alpha system, regardless of their species or origin.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18166.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log 18166.3-1:</b> On ██/██/20██, a team of Foundation personnel was dispatched to <i>SCP-18166</i>-1 in order to observe and study the creature. Upon arriving, the team was immediately attacked by <i>SCP-18166</i>; however, all attempts to harm or subdue it were unsuccessful. After several minutes of observation, the team determined that SCP 18166 appeared to be protecting something within its vicinity and withdrew from the area without further incident.</div>
