<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-21489 is  a wand that turns the user into a mahou shoujo, aka a magical girl. (regardless of gender)</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-21489<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-21489</i> is a wooden wand, measuring approximately 30 cm in length and 2.5 cm in diameter. It has been painted white with pink and yellow stripes running along its length. The handle of the wand is decorated with several small stars made from silver foil.<br><br>When held by a human subject, <i>SCP-21489</i> will cause them to become a mahou shoujo (magical girl). This transformation occurs gradually over the course of one hour, during which time the subject's physical appearance will change to match that of a stereotypical magical girl character from Japanese media; this includes changes such as hair color and style, clothing style, and facial features (allowing for some variation based on individual preference). Additionally, subjects gain access to supernatural abilities related to their chosen magical girl archetype; these abilities are typically themed around light or protection magic.<br><br>Once transformed into a mahou shoujo state, subjects remain in this form until they willingly relinquish it by placing <i>SCP-21489</i> back into storage or destroying it entirely. Subjects who have undergone this transformation report feeling an increased sense of self confidence and purpose when using their newfound powers for good causes.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-21489</i> is to be kept in a secure locker at Site-██ when not in use. Access to <i>SCP-21489</i> is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any personnel who come into contact with <i>SCP-21489</i> must be monitored for signs of transformation and immediately placed under observation if any are detected. Subjects affected by <i>SCP-21489</i> must be contained until the transformation has completed, at which point they may be released back into the general population provided their behavior does not pose a threat to Foundation interests.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21489.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-21489</i> was recovered from a small shop in Tokyo, Japan. The owner of the shop claimed to have no knowledge of the wand's origin or effects, but stated that it had been in her possession for several years.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21489.2: </h3> Interview with Subject D-21489<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████████<br><b>Subject:</b> D-21489, a female subject who had recently been transformed by <i>SCP-21489</i>.<br>&ltBegin Log&gt<br><b>Dr. ████████:</b> Good morning, D-21489. Can you tell me about your experience with <i>SCP-21489</i>? <br><b>D-21489:</b> It was… strange. I felt like something was happening to me, but I couldn't quite put my finger on it. My body started changing and then suddenly I was wearing this really cute outfit and had these amazing powers! It felt like a dream come true! <br><b>Dr. ████████:</b> What kind of powers did you gain? <br><b>D-21489:</b> I could create shields of light to protect people, and shoot beams of energy from my hands. It was really cool! <br><b>Dr. ████████:</b> Did you feel any different after the transformation? <br><b>D-21489:</b> Yeah, definitely. I felt more confident and like I had a purpose in life. It was like I had been given this amazing gift and it was up to me to use it for good. <br>&ltEnd Log&gt<br><b>Closing Statement:</b> D-21489 was returned to her cell after the interview and remains under observation.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21489.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Test 1:</b> Subject D-1789 was instructed to hold <i>SCP-21489</i> for one hour. At the end of the hour, the subject had undergone a full transformation into a mahou shoujo state. The subject reported feeling an increased sense of confidence and purpose when using their newfound powers for good causes.<br><b><br>Test 2:</b> Subject D-5678 was instructed to hold <i>SCP-21489</i> for two hours. At the end of this time, the subject had not yet completed their transformation and began exhibiting signs of distress due to prolonged exposure to <i>SCP-21489</i>'s effects. The experiment was terminated early and the subject returned to baseline after being removed from contact with <i>SCP-21489</i>.</div>
