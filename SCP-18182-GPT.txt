<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18182 is  Me wishing the creators of this site a nice day :)</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18182<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18182</i> is an instance of a thought expressed by an unknown individual. The thought reads as follows: <i>"I wish the creators of this site a nice day."</i><br><br>When <i>SCP-18182</i> is expressed, all individuals within earshot experience a positive emotional state, regardless of their current emotional state or the context in which it was said. This effect lasts for approximately one hour after expression before fading away.<br><br>The source of <i>SCP-18182</i>'s anomalous properties are currently unknown.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18182</i> is to be contained in a standard containment locker at Site-██. Access to <i>SCP-18182</i> is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any personnel who express <i>SCP-18182</i> are to be monitored for any unusual behavior and given psychological counseling if necessary.<br><br>In the event of a containment breach, all personnel within earshot of <i>SCP-18182</i> are to be administered Class B amnestics and monitored for any further anomalous effects.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18182.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, <i>SCP-18182</i> was expressed by a researcher at Site-██. Upon expression of the thought, all personnel within earshot experienced a heightened sense of well-being and reported increased productivity for the remainder of their shift.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18182.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-18182</i><br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> <i>SCP-18182</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████████<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> Can you tell me anything about yourself?<br><b><br><i>SCP-18182</i>:</b> I'm just a thought. It's hard to explain, but I know that I exist and that I have the power to make people feel better. I don't know why, but I like it.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18182.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Test #:</b> 18182-1<br><b><br>Subject:</b> D-9876<br><b><br>Procedure:</b> Subject was instructed to express <i>SCP-18182</i> aloud.<br><b><br>Result:</b> Subject experienced a positive emotional state for approximately one hour before returning to their baseline emotional state. No other anomalous effects were observed.</div>
