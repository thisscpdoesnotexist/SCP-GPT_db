<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-24523 is  A cat that can mind control people while they're pooping</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-24523<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-24523</i> is a domestic shorthair cat of indeterminate age and gender. It has no distinguishing features or markings, other than its white fur.<br><br>When an individual is in the act of defecating, <i>SCP-24523</i> will enter the room and begin to meow loudly. Upon hearing this sound, the individual will be compelled to obey any verbal commands given by <i>SCP-24523</i>. This effect lasts until either the individual finishes their bowel movement or leaves the room; whichever comes first.<br><br><i>SCP-24523</i> does not appear to understand any language other than English, but it can communicate with individuals through gestures and body language. It does not appear to have any malicious intent when using its powers; rather it seems content simply being able to influence people's behavior in such a manner.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br><i>SCP-24523</i> is to be kept in a standard containment chamber at Site-██. The chamber should have a litter box, food and water dishes, and toys for the cat's entertainment. Personnel are not to enter the chamber without permission from an on-site supervisor.<br><br>In order to prevent accidental activation of <i>SCP-24523</i>'s anomalous properties, personnel must not defecate within its line of sight or within earshot. If this occurs, personnel must immediately leave the room until <i>SCP-24523</i> has ceased meowing.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24523.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/████, <i>SCP-24523</i> was found in the restroom of a local fast food restaurant. It had been meowing at customers and making them do various tasks, such as cleaning the floors or washing dishes. The incident was contained without any casualties or injuries.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24523.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-24523</i><br><br>On ██/██/████, personnel conducted an interview with <i>SCP-24523</i>. The following is a transcript of the conversation:<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Hello there, <i>SCP-24523</i>. Can you tell us why you do what you do?<br><b><br><i>SCP-24523</i>:</b> [Meows] I just like to be in control. It's fun!<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Do you understand what it is that you're doing?<br><b><br><i>SCP-24523</i>:</b> [Meows] Of course I do! It's not like I'm some kind of mindless animal, after all.<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Do you have any other abilities?<br><b><br><i>SCP-24523</i>:</b> [Meows] No, I don't think so. This is the only thing I can do.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24523.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Test #:</b> 24523-1<br><b>Subject:</b> D-25663, a male human of average physical strength.<br><b>Procedure:</b> Subject was instructed to enter the containment chamber and defecate in the provided toilet. <i>SCP-24523</i> was then released into the room.<br><b>Results:</b> As soon as <i>SCP-24523</i> entered the room, it began meowing loudly. Upon hearing this sound, subject immediately ceased his bowel movement and complied with all verbal commands given by <i>SCP-24523</i> for approximately three minutes before being allowed to leave the chamber.</div>
