It stands approximately 3 meters tall and weighs around 2 metric tons. The body of the Maneki-neko is constructed from reinforced steel and its arms are equipped with two high caliber machine guns. Its eyes glow red when active, and it has been observed to move at speeds of up to 30 km/h.<br><br><i>SCP-21267</i>'s primary purpose appears to be defense; it will actively seek out potential threats and attack them with its weapons if they come within range. In addition, it possesses an array of advanced sensors which allow it to detect hostile entities in the vicinity even when not visible or audible. When activated, <i>SCP-21267</i> will begin patrolling the area in search of any potential targets for destruction or capture using whatever means necessary (including lethal force).<br><br>When inactive, <i>SCP-21267</i> appears dormant until disturbed by external stimuli such as sound or movement; upon activation, its eyes will light up red and it will become active again until all perceived threats have been neutralized or evaded successfully.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-21267</i> is to be kept in a secure containment chamber at Site-██. The chamber must be equipped with motion sensors and sound dampening technology to prevent accidental activation of the entity. Access to the containment chamber is restricted to personnel with Level 4 clearance or higher.<br><br>In order to prevent unauthorized access, all personnel entering or leaving the containment chamber must pass through a security checkpoint manned by two armed guards at all times. Any attempts at tampering with <i>SCP-21267</i>'s containment systems will result in immediate termination of those responsible.<br><br>In addition, any personnel found attempting to activate <i>SCP-21267</i> without authorization will be subject to disciplinary action up to and including termination from service.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21267.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, <i>SCP-21267</i> was discovered by Foundation personnel in a warehouse owned by the Yakuza.
