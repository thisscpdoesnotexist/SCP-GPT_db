The effect is not limited to statements made by the speaker, and can be used to make any prior statement true regardless of its veracity.<br><br>The effects of <i>SCP-18747</i> are instantaneous and cannot be reversed or negated in any way. Furthermore, attempting to use <i>SCP-18747</i> on a statement that was already false will result in an unpredictable outcome with potentially catastrophic results.<br><br>Due to the potential for misuse and abuse of this anomaly, all personnel must adhere strictly to Protocol 18747 at all times when interacting with <i>SCP-18747</i>. Any attempts at circumventing or ignoring Protocol 18747 will result in immediate disciplinary action up to and including termination from Foundation employment.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18747</i> is to be contained in a secure locker at Site ██. Access to <i>SCP-18747</i> is restricted to personnel with Level 4 clearance or higher, and all interactions with <i>SCP-18747</i> must be conducted under the supervision of a senior researcher.<br><br>Any attempts at using <i>SCP-18747</i> outside of approved testing protocols must be reported immediately to the Site Director.
