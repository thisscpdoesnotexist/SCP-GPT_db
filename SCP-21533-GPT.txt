<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-21533 is  a god, but also a duck</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-21533<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-21533</i> is an entity that appears to be a combination of a deity and a duck. It has the physical form of a large, white duck with black eyes, but it also possesses divine powers.<br><br><i>SCP-21533</i>'s primary abilities are related to manipulating reality in various ways. It can create objects or entities out of thin air, alter existing objects and entities, and manipulate time and space itself. <i>SCP-21533</i> can also manipulate emotions and memories in individuals it comes into contact with.<br><br>In addition to its reality manipulation abilities, <i>SCP-21533</i> is capable of speaking any language fluently as well as understanding all languages spoken around it. When questioned about its origin or purpose, however, <i>SCP-21533</i> will not respond or provide any information beyond what has already been observed during containment procedures.<br><br><i>SCP-21533</i> is hostile towards humans but docile towards other avian species such as ducks and geese; when confronted by humans it will attempt to escape if possible or attack if cornered. If left undisturbed for extended periods of time (typically several hours), <i>SCP-21533</i> becomes increasingly agitated until eventually attacking anything nearby regardless of species or affiliation.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-21533</i> is to be contained in a standard avian containment unit at Site-██. The containment unit should be equipped with an automated feeder system and monitored by security personnel at all times.<br><br>In the event of a containment breach, MTF units are to be dispatched immediately to recapture <i>SCP-21533</i> using nonlethal force if possible. If lethal force is deemed necessary, MTF units are authorized to use tranquilizer darts or other nonlethal methods of subduing <i>SCP-21533</i> before returning it to its containment unit.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21533.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-21533</i> was observed to be in a state of distress. When approached by personnel, it began speaking in an unknown language and gesturing towards the sky. After several minutes of this behavior, <i>SCP-21533</i> flew away from Site-██ and has not been seen since.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21533.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-21533</i><br><b><br><b>Interviewed: </b> <i>SCP-21533</i><br><b><br></b> <b>Interviewer: </b> Dr. ████<br><b><br></b> <br> Foreword:</b> Interview conducted in the containment unit of <i>SCP-21533</i>. The entity was uncooperative and refused to answer most questions posed by the interviewer.<br><br>&ltBegin Log&gt<br><b><br>Dr. ████:</b> Hello, <i>SCP-21533</i>. Can you tell me anything about yourself?<br><b><br><i>SCP-21533</i>:</b> (silence)<br><b><br>Dr. ████:</b> Are you a god? A duck? Something else entirely? Please answer the question. <br><b><br> <i>SCP-21533</i>:</b> I am both a god and a duck, but I cannot say more than that.<br><b><br>Dr. ████:</b> Why not? <br><b><br><i>SCP-21533</i>:</b> It is not my place to reveal such information. <br><br>&ltEnd Log&gt<br><b><br>Closing Statement:</b> <i>SCP-21533</i> refused to answer any further questions and the interview was terminated.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21533.3: </h3> Experiment Logs<br><b><br>Experiment 21533-A:</b> <i>SCP-21533</i> was presented with a small toy duck and instructed to create a duplicate. After several minutes of concentration, <i>SCP-21533</i> created an exact copy of the toy duck. The duplicate was then removed from the containment unit for further study.<br><b><br>Results:</b> Successful duplication of object. Duplicate is nonanomalous and indistinguishable from original object in all respects.</div>
