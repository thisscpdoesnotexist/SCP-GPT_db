Despite this, it can still be accessed via any standard web browser or email client.<br><br>When accessed, <i>SCP-18738</i> displays an automated message from the Foundation regarding its purpose and usage guidelines, along with contact information for further inquiries. No other content is available on the site.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18738</i> is to be monitored by Foundation personnel for any suspicious activity.
