<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-21599 is  a can of bepis</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-21599<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-21599</i> is a can of an unknown carbonated beverage, labeled <i>"Bepis"</i>. The contents of the can are not known, as it cannot be opened. Attempts to open <i>SCP-21599</i> have resulted in the destruction of any tool used to attempt opening it.<br><br>The anomalous properties of <i>SCP-21599</i> become apparent when any living creature comes within 1 meter (3 feet) of the object. Any creature that comes within this range will immediately experience intense physical pain and disorientation followed by unconsciousness for 10 minutes. After regaining consciousness, subjects report feeling extreme hunger and thirst which cannot be alleviated by eating or drinking anything else other than what is contained inside the can. Subjects who consume what is inside <i>SCP-21599</i> will experience intense euphoria followed by death shortly after consumption due to severe organ failure caused by an unknown toxin present in the liquid.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-21599</i> is to be stored in a secure containment locker at Site-██. Access to the object is restricted to personnel with Level 4 clearance or higher. Any personnel who come into contact with <i>SCP-21599</i> must be monitored for signs of distress and immediately removed from the area if any are observed.<br><br>In the event of a containment breach, all personnel within 1 meter (3 feet) of <i>SCP-21599</i> must be evacuated immediately and placed under observation for 72 hours.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21599.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, an experiment was conducted in which a robotic arm was used to attempt to open <i>SCP-21599</i>. The arm successfully opened the can and revealed a bright blue liquid inside. Analysis of the liquid revealed it to be composed of various unknown chemicals, some of which are believed to be neurotoxins. Further testing is ongoing.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21599.2: </h3> Interview with Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> D-74225<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Please state your name and number for the record. <br><b>D-74225:</b> My name is [REDACTED], and my number is D-74225. <br><b>Dr. ██████:</b> Alright, D-74225, I need to ask you some questions about <i>SCP-21599</i>.
