<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i><i>SCP-16869</i> is  A Glock-17 that when fired, will fire a random object, existing object or not</i> </h3> </center><br><h3>Item #:</h3> <i>SCP-16869</i><br><br><h3>Object Class:</h3> Safe<br><br><h3>Special Containment Procedures:</h3> <i>SCP-16869</i> is to be kept in a standard containment locker. Any objects fired by <i>SCP-16869</i> are to be contained and studied.<br><br><h3>Description:</h3> <i>SCP-16869</i> is a Glock-17 pistol. When fired, <i>SCP-16869</i> will fire an object that it has been exposed to. This can be any object, including non-existent objects and abstract concepts.<br><br><i>SCP-16869</i> was recovered in ████████, ████ after a police raid on a drug lab found it in the possession of one of the men arrested. The man claimed that he had purchased the gun from an online auction site and had no idea how it worked or why he needed it. Further investigation revealed that <i>SCP-16869</i> had been sold to him by another man who claimed to have found it at his local church .<br><br><h3>Addendum 16869.1 : </h3><br><br><h3>Addendum 16869.2 : </h3>Interview with ██████ ██████<br><br><b><b>Interviewed: </b> ██████ ██████, owner of <i>SCP-16869</i> </b> <b><br><br><b>Interviewer: </b> Dr. ██████████ </b><br><br><b>Foreword: </b>Mr. ██████ had been arrested for possession of marijuana and was being questioned about his knowledge of <i>SCP-16869</i>. <br><br>&lt;Begin Log&gt<br><br><b>Dr. ██████████: </b>Alright, Mr. ██████, I'm going to ask you a few questions about the gun you were arrested with earlier today. Can you tell me how it works? <br><br><b>Mr. ██████: </b>It's a Glock 17, it's automatic and has seventeen rounds in the magazine and one in the chamber so 18 total right? <br><br><b>Dr. ██████████: </b>Right… And do you know how it works? How does it fire? <br><br><b>Mr. ██████: </b>I don't know man, I just bought it off this guy online who said he found it at his local church or something like that but he was selling them cheap because they didn't work or something like that so I figured why not buy one and see what happens when I fire it so yeah that's pretty much all there is to say really… Dr. ██████████ pauses for several seconds before continuing the interview <br><br>&lt;End Log&gt<br><br> Closing <br><br><b>Statement: </b>Further questioning revealed no additional information regarding <i>SCP-16869</i><br><br><h3>Addendum 16869.3 : </h3>Experiment Log<br><br>Experiment 16869-1:<br><br>Object <br><br><b>fired: </b>A standard playing card.<br><br><b>Result: </b>A standard playing card.<br><br>Experiment 16869-2:<br><br>Object <br><br><b>fired: </b>The object <i>SCP-16869</i> was found with, a crucifix.<br><br><b>Result: </b>An exact replica of the crucifix, except made of plastic instead of metal and painted gold instead of silver.
