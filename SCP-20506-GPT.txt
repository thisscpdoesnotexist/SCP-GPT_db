<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-20506 is  a giant bronze statue of dick tracy that comes to life whenever trouble's afoot</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-20506<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Thaumiel<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-20506</i> is a giant bronze statue of the comic book character Dick Tracy, standing approximately 30 meters tall. The statue is made from an unknown metal alloy and appears to be indestructible.<br><br>When trouble arises in the vicinity of <i>SCP-20506</i>, it will animate and move towards the source of the problem. It will then attempt to resolve the situation using its superhuman strength, agility, and combat prowess. Once it has resolved or contained the issue at hand, <i>SCP-20506</i> will return to its original position until further disturbances arise in its vicinity.<br><br><i>SCP-20506</i> also possesses several other supernatural abilities such as flight, teleportation, shape shifting and advanced healing capabilities that allow it to survive even fatal injuries with no lasting effects. Additionally it can communicate telepathically with any sentient being within a 500 meter radius of itself.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-20506</i> is to be kept in a secure containment chamber at Site-██. The chamber should be monitored by security personnel at all times and access to the area should only be granted with Level 4 clearance or higher.<br><br>In the event of an emergency, <i>SCP-20506</i> may be released from its containment chamber to assist in resolving the situation. After it has completed its mission, it must immediately return to its containment chamber and remain there until further notice.<br><br>Any attempts to control or manipulate <i>SCP-20506</i> are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disciplinary action.<br><br><h3> Addendum 20506.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-20506</i> was deployed to assist in the containment of a group of hostile entities that had breached Site-██. After successfully containing the threat, <i>SCP-20506</i> returned to its containment chamber and has remained there since.<br><br><h3> Addendum 20506.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-20506</i><br><br>On ██/██/████, Dr. ███████ conducted an interview with <i>SCP-20506</i> to gain further insight into its abilities and origin. The following is a transcript of the conversation:<br><b><br>Dr. ███████:</b> Hello, <i>SCP-20506</i>. Can you tell us anything about yourself?<br><b><br><i>SCP-20506</i>:</b> I am Dick Tracy, defender of justice and protector of the innocent. I have been around for many years, but my purpose remains unchanged - to fight crime wherever it may be found and bring peace to those in need.<br><b><br>Dr. ███████:</b> How long have you been in this form?<br><b><br><i>SCP-20506</i>:</b> I do not remember exactly, but it has been many years since I first assumed this form.<br><b><br>Dr. ███████:</b> What is your purpose?<br><b><br><i>SCP-20506</i>:</b> My primary purpose is to protect the innocent and stop crime wherever it may be found. I also serve as a symbol of justice and hope for those who are in need of help.<br><br><h3> Addendum 20506.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Date:</b> ██/██/████<br><b><br>Subject:</b> D-20506-1, a former police officer with extensive combat training.<br><b><br>Procedure:</b> D-20506-1 was instructed to attack <i>SCP-20506</i> in an attempt to subdue it. The subject was armed with standard issue firearms and body armor.<br><b><br>Result:</b> <i>SCP-20506</i> effortlessly defeated the subject using its superior strength and agility, despite being outnumbered and outgunned. After subduing the subject, it returned to its original position without further incident.</div>
