<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-25631 is  just lazy wish fulfillment tbh</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-25631<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Thaumiel<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-25631</i> is a reality-warping entity capable of granting the wishes of those who interact with it. It appears as an incorporeal, humanoid figure that can manifest itself in any location, typically appearing to its target as a person they are familiar with or admire.<br><br>When interacted with, <i>SCP-25631</i> will ask for the individual's wish and then proceed to grant it within a matter of seconds. The effects created by these wishes are permanent and cannot be reversed or altered in any way.<br><br>Despite its seemingly benevolent nature, <i>SCP-25631</i> has been known to grant malicious wishes if requested by individuals wishing harm upon others. As such, extreme caution should be taken when interacting with this entity.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-25631</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. All personnel interacting with <i>SCP-25631</i> must be accompanied by at least two security guards and all requests for wishes must be approved by the site director before being granted.<br><br>In order to prevent any potential misuse of its powers, all personnel interacting with <i>SCP-25631</i> are to undergo psychological evaluation prior to and after each interaction. Any personnel found to have malicious intent or ill will towards others are not allowed access to <i>SCP-25631</i> under any circumstances.<br><br><h3> Addendum 25631.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, a containment breach occurred at Site-██ due to an individual attempting to use <i>SCP-25631</i> for personal gain.
