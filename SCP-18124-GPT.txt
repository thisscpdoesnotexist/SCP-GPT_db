The entity's head is featureless except for two large eyes which are constantly glowing with a bright yellow light.<br><br><i>SCP-18124</i> displays sapience and sentience, being able to communicate through spoken words in multiple languages including English and Spanish. It can also alter its own shape at will, allowing it to assume any form desired by itself or those around it.<br><br>When provoked or threatened <i>SCP-18124</i> will become hostile towards whatever threat presents itself; however if left undisturbed it will usually remain passive unless engaged in discourse about matters related to its mission statement.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18124</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██.
