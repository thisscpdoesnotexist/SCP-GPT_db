<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-20665 is  I will make you shed your tears!</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-20665<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-20665</i> is a small, plush toy in the shape of a rabbit. The rabbit has large, blue eyes and a wide smile with two long ears. When an individual comes into contact with <i>SCP-20665</i>, they will begin to cry uncontrollably. The crying will not cease until the individual is at least five meters away from <i>SCP-20665</i>.<br><br>The tears shed by individuals affected by <i>SCP-20665</i> are not caused by sadness or grief but instead appear to be caused by joy and happiness. Individuals affected have reported feeling joyous and content after shedding their tears while in close proximity to <i>SCP-20665</i>.<br><br>When removed from its containment chamber, <i>SCP-20665</i> appears to lose its anomalous properties; however, if placed back within it, the effects return immediately upon contact with any living creature within five meters of it.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-20665</i> is to be kept in a standard containment chamber at all times and monitored by security cameras. No personnel are to come into contact with <i>SCP-20665</i> without proper authorization from the Site Director.<br><br>In the event of a containment breach, all personnel must evacuate the area immediately and notify security personnel. If any personnel are found to have been affected by <i>SCP-20665</i>’s anomalous properties, they must be debriefed and given appropriate psychological counseling as soon as possible.<br><br><h3> Addendum 20665.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/████, a small group of researchers attempted to interact with <i>SCP-20665</i> in order to learn more about its anomalous properties. After several minutes of interaction, it became apparent that the object was responding to their presence and was attempting to communicate with them. The following is an excerpt from the transcript of their conversation:<br><b><br><i>SCP-20665</i>:</b> “I will make you shed your tears!”<br><b><br>Researcher 1:</b> “What do you mean?” <br><b><br><i>SCP-20665</i>:</b> “Your tears are a sign of joy. Shed them and feel my love.”<br><br><h3> Addendum 20665.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-20665</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████████<br><b>Interviewee:</b> <i>SCP-20665</i><br>&ltBegin Log&gt<br><b>Dr. ████████:</b> Hello, <i>SCP-20665</i>. Can you tell me why you make people cry? <br><b><i>SCP-20665</i>:</b> I want to bring joy and happiness to those around me! When someone is sad or feeling down, I want them to know that they are loved and appreciated. That's why I make them shed tears of joy!<br><br><h3> Addendum 20665.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log 20665.3:</b> On ██/██/████, <i>SCP-20665</i> was placed in a room with two D-Class personnel and instructed to make contact with it. Upon doing so, both D-Class personnel began to cry uncontrollably for approximately five minutes before the effects ceased. Both reported feeling joyous and content afterwards.</div>
