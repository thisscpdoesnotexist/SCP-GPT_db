<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17588 is  A red flag that becomes blue when a person is minimum 100 metres away from it</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17588<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17588</i> is a red flag measuring two meters in length, with a white star in the center. It is made of an unknown material and cannot be damaged by any means.<br><br>When a person approaches within 100 meters of <i>SCP-17588</i>, it will change to blue. This effect will remain until the person moves away from the object or is no longer visible to it. If multiple people are present within 100 meters of <i>SCP-17588</i>, each individual must move out of range before its color changes back to red.<br><br>If left unattended for more than 24 hours, <i>SCP-17588</i> will begin to emit an audio signal that increases in intensity as time passes until someone moves out of range or is no longer visible to it. The sound has been described as similar to a high pitched whistle and can be heard up to 400m away from the object's location.<br><br>Testing has revealed that when exposed directly to sunlight, <i>SCP-17588</i>'s color does not change despite any number of people being present within 100m radius around it; however if removed from direct sunlight the color changing effect resumes immediately upon reentry into viewable range.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17588</i> is to be kept in a secure containment locker at Site-██. Access to the object is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any personnel approaching within 100 meters of <i>SCP-17588</i> are required to wear protective gear and remain aware of the object's color changing effect. Personnel must also refrain from making any sudden movements, as this could trigger an audio signal from <i>SCP-17588</i>.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17588.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-17588</i> was found in the possession of a group of individuals claiming to be members of an organization known as <i>"The Flag Bearers"</i>. The group claimed that they were using <i>SCP-17588</i> to mark their territory and ward off trespassers. All members were apprehended and interrogated; however no further information could be obtained from them.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17588.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-17588</i><br><br>On ██/██/20██, personnel at Site-██ conducted an interview with <i>SCP-17588</i> in order to gain further insight into its properties. The following conversation was recorded:<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> What is your purpose?<br><b><br><i>SCP-17588</i>:</b> My purpose is to alert people when they are too close.<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> What happens when people come too close?<br><b><br><i>SCP-17588</i>:</b> I will change color to blue and emit a warning sound.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17588.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Test #:</b> 1<br><b>Subject:</b> D-17588-1<br><b>Procedure:</b> D-17588-1 was instructed to approach <i>SCP-17588</i> from a distance of 10 meters and observe the object's color change.<br><b>Results:</b> Upon reaching the 10 meter mark, <i>SCP-17588</i> changed from red to blue. D-17588-1 reported feeling a slight tingling sensation in his hands and feet as he moved closer. The color remained blue until he moved out of view or beyond 100 meters away from the object.</div>
