<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17334 is  A flying bird</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17334<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17334</i> is an avian entity of unknown species, approximately the size of a common raven. It has no visible wings, yet it is able to fly at speeds up to 40 km/h (25 mph). Its body is composed entirely of feathers and its eyes appear to be made out of solid gold.<br><br>When in flight, <i>SCP-17334</i> emits a low humming noise that can be heard from up to 10 meters away. When approached by humans or other living creatures, <i>SCP-17334</i> will cease all activity and remain stationary until the creature leaves its vicinity. If agitated or threatened, it will attempt to flee the area with extreme speed and agility.<br><br><i>SCP-17334</i> does not appear to require sustenance as it has never been observed eating or drinking anything during containment. It appears content with simply flying around its enclosure for extended periods of time without pause.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17334</i> is to be contained in a standard aviary at Site-██. The enclosure should be large enough for it to fly freely and contain several perches for it to rest on.<br><br>The enclosure should also have an automated system that will close the doors if <i>SCP-17334</i> attempts to leave the area. Personnel are not allowed to enter the aviary without permission from a Level 3 or higher personnel.<br><br>Any attempts by personnel to interact with <i>SCP-17334</i> must first be approved by a Level 4 personnel, and all interactions must take place under supervision of at least two security guards.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17334.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, <i>SCP-17334</i> was observed hovering in the air for an extended period of time before emitting a loud screeching noise. Afterward, it flew away from its enclosure and out of sight.<br><br>It has not been seen since and is presumed to have escaped containment.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17334.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-17334</i><br><b><br><b>Interviewed: </b> <i>SCP-17334</i> </b> <b>Interviewer:  Dr. ████ </b> <br> Foreword: The following interview was conducted in order to gain a better understanding of the entity's behavior and origin. &ltBegin Log&gt Dr. ████: Can you tell me your name? <i>SCP-17334</i>: [silence] Dr. ████: Do you understand me? <i>SCP-17334</i>: [nods] Dr. ████: What is your name? <i>SCP-17334</i>: I do not have one…I am just here…Drifting through the skies… &ltEnd Log&gt Closing Statement: Further attempts at communication with the entity were unsuccessful, as it refused to answer any further questions or respond to any stimuli.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17334.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment 1:</b> <i>SCP-17334</i> was placed in a large room with several other avian species. After 3 hours of observation, it was observed to be the only one not engaging in any type of social activity with the other birds.<br><b><br>Conclusion:</b> <i>SCP-17334</i> appears to prefer solitude and does not interact well with other creatures.</div>
