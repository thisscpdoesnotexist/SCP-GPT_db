<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18007 is  a racecar guaranteed to place second in any race. No matter how contrived the circumstances need to be to ensure that happens.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18007<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18007</i> is a white and red racecar, with no visible manufacturer markings. It appears to be in perfect condition, despite its age.<br><br>When placed in any racing competition, <i>SCP-18007</i> will always place second. No matter how contrived the circumstances need to be to ensure that happens; whether it is through sabotaging other racers or using supernatural means, <i>SCP-18007</i> will always come in second place.<br><br>The driver of <i>SCP-18007</i> has reported feeling an intense urge to win the race while driving it, but they are unable to do so no matter what they do. The driver also reports feeling a sense of relief when they inevitably come in second place after trying their hardest to win the race.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br><i>SCP-18007</i> is to be stored in a secure garage at Site-██. Access to <i>SCP-18007</i> is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Testing of <i>SCP-18007</i> must be approved by the O5 Council before it can take place. Any testing involving other vehicles must also be approved by the O5 Council, and all drivers involved in such tests must sign a waiver acknowledging that they are aware of the anomalous properties of <i>SCP-18007</i> and that they understand that they may not win the race no matter what they do.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18007.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-18007</i> was tested in a race against two other vehicles. The results were as expected; <i>SCP-18007</i> came in second place, despite the other drivers attempting to sabotage it.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18007.2: </h3> Interview with Driver<br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> Driver of <i>SCP-18007</i>, ████████ ██████████<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ███████<br><b><br>Dr. ███████:</b> So, tell me about your experience driving <i>SCP-18007</i>?<br><b><br>Driver ██████████:</b> Well, it was… strange. I had this overwhelming urge to win the race, but no matter what I did, I couldn't seem to do it. It was like something was pushing me back and making sure that I always came in second place. It was almost like it wanted me to lose.<br><b><br>Dr. ███████:</b> Did you feel any other effects while driving <i>SCP-18007</i>?<br><b><br>Driver ██████████:</b> Yeah, I felt a sense of relief when I came in second place. Like something inside me was happy that I hadn't won the race. It was weird, but it wasn't unpleasant or anything.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18007.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Test #1:</b> <i>SCP-18007</i> was entered into a race with five other vehicles. All drivers were aware of the anomalous properties of <i>SCP-18007</i> and signed waivers acknowledging this. Despite all efforts, <i>SCP-18007</i> came in second place at the end of the race.<br><b><br>Test #2:</b> <i>SCP-18007</i> was entered into a race with ten other vehicles, none of which were aware of its anomalous properties. Despite all efforts, <i>SCP-18007</i> still came in second place at the end of the race.</div>
