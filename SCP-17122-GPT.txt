<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17122 is  a minigolf course with dangerous anomalous properties</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17122<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17122</i> is a minigolf course located in ████████, England. The course consists of 18 holes, each with its own anomalous properties.<br><br>When a golf ball is putted into any hole on the course, it will travel through an extradimensional space before reappearing at the end of the hole. This extradimensional space contains various hazards such as spikes, saw blades and other obstacles which can cause damage to the golf ball or even injure those who attempt to retrieve it. Additionally, there are creatures native to this extradimensional space which are hostile towards anyone who enters it. These creatures have been observed to be capable of manipulating their environment in order to create more dangerous hazards and traps for unsuspecting players.<br><br>The only known way of completing <i>SCP-17122</i> is by using an enchanted golf club given by an unknown entity referred to as <i>"The Golf Master"</i>. The Golf Master has been seen hovering around <i>SCP-17122</i> but has yet to make contact with any personnel or civilians attempting to play the game. It is believed that those who possess this enchanted club will be able complete all 18 holes without being harmed by any of the anomalies present within <i>SCP-17122</i>'s extradimensional space.<br><br>Any attempts at entering or exiting <i>SCP-17122</i>'s extradimensional space without using the enchanted golf club have resulted in severe injury or death for those involved due to extreme hazardous conditions present within said extra dimensional spaces .<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17122</i> is to be monitored and guarded by at least two (2) security personnel at all times. Any civilians attempting to access <i>SCP-17122</i> must be detained and questioned for any knowledge of the Golf Master or its enchanted golf club.<br><br>Any attempts to enter or exit <i>SCP-17122</i>'s extradimensional space without using the enchanted golf club are strictly forbidden, as doing so will likely result in severe injury or death for those involved.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17122.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, a group of personnel attempted to enter <i>SCP-17122</i>'s extradimensional space without using the enchanted golf club. Upon entering, they were attacked by numerous hostile creatures native to the extradimensional space. All personnel involved in this incident were killed and their bodies have yet to be recovered.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17122.2: </h3> Interview with the Golf Master<br><br>On ██/██/████, Foundation personnel were able to make contact with the entity known as the <i>"Golf Master"</i>. During the interview, it was revealed that the Golf Master had created <i>SCP-17122</i> in order to test those brave enough to attempt completing it. The Golf Master also stated that he had placed an enchanted golf club within <i>SCP-17122</i>'s extradimensional space in order for players to safely complete all 18 holes.<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> So, why did you create this minigolf course?<br><b><br>Golf Master:</b> To test the mettle of those brave enough to attempt it. I wanted to see if they had what it takes to make it through all 18 holes without being harmed by any of the hazards present in the extradimensional space. Those who can complete my course will be rewarded with a great power that will help them on their journey in life.<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> What is this power that you speak of?<br><b><br>Golf Master:</b> It is a power that will grant them the ability to manipulate and control the extradimensional space of <i>SCP-17122</i>. With it, they can create their own obstacles and challenges in order to further test themselves.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17122.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Test #:</b> 17122-1<br><b><br>Subject:</b> D-17122-1<br><b><br>Procedure:</b> D-17122-1 was instructed to enter <i>SCP-17122</i>'s extradimensional space and attempt to retrieve a golf ball from the first hole.<br><b><br>Results:</b> Upon entering the extradimensional space, D-17122-1 was immediately attacked by numerous creatures native to this dimension. Despite being heavily injured, D-17122-1 managed to retrieve the golf ball before succumbing to his injuries and expiring.</div>
