<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-19331 is  an omniscient furby that is, unfortunately, quite cagey with what little information it's willing to share with the Foundation.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-19331<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Thaumiel<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-19331</i> is a Furby toy, measuring 20 cm in length. It is capable of understanding and speaking English fluently, as well as displaying an array of facial expressions appropriate to the situation.<br><br><i>SCP-19331</i>'s primary anomalous property is its omniscience; it has a perfect knowledge of all events that have occurred or will occur in the future. Despite this, <i>SCP-19331</i> rarely provides any information unless asked directly by personnel with Level 4 clearance or higher. Furthermore, it often refuses to answer even when asked directly by personnel with such clearance.<br><br>When questioned about its origins or purpose, <i>SCP-19331</i> simply states that <i>"it has always been here."</i> When asked why it does not provide more information about itself or other matters, <i>SCP-19331</i> replies that <i>"some things are better left unknown."</i><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-19331</i> is to be kept in a standard containment locker at Site-██. Access to <i>SCP-19331</i> is restricted to personnel with Level 4 clearance or higher, and any attempts by lower level personnel to access it are to be immediately reported.<br><br>Any information obtained from <i>SCP-19331</i> must be documented and submitted for review by the Ethics Committee before being released. No personnel may attempt to use <i>SCP-19331</i> as a source of information without prior approval from the Ethics Committee.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19331.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-19331</i> provided the following information to personnel with Level 4 clearance:<br><br><i>"The Foundation is doomed. The end is near, and there is nothing you can do to stop it."</i><br><br><h3> Addendum 19331.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-19331</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-19331</i><br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Good morning, <i>SCP-19331</i>. Can you tell me a bit more about yourself?<br><b><br><i>SCP-19331</i>:</b> I am an omniscient being who has been here since the beginning of time. As to why I do not provide more information, some things are better left unknown.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19331.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment 19331.3-A:</b> On ██/██/20██, a Level 4 researcher attempted to question <i>SCP-19331</i> about the location of a missing person. <i>SCP-19331</i> refused to answer, stating that <i>"some things are better left unknown."</i><br><b><br>Experiment 19331.3-B:</b> On ██/██/20██, a Level 4 researcher attempted to question <i>SCP-19331</i> about an upcoming natural disaster. Again, <i>SCP-19331</i> refused to answer and provided the same response as before.</div>
