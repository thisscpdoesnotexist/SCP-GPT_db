<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-21824 is  A pigeon that hates everything and will argue with anything he comes across</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-21824<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-21824</i> is a white-feathered pigeon of indeterminate age and gender. Its wingspan is approximately 0.5m, with an average weight of 0.2kg.<br><br><i>SCP-21824</i> displays a highly aggressive attitude towards any living thing it encounters, including personnel and other animals. It will often vocalize its displeasure with loud squawks or caws that can be heard up to 30 meters away from the subject's location.<br><br>When <i>SCP-21824</i> perceives an individual as having wronged it in some way, it will actively pursue them until they are out of sight or have left the area where <i>SCP-21824</i> resides (see Addendum 21824/1). When confronted by personnel, the subject will attempt to defend itself using its beak and claws; however, these attacks are generally ineffective against human skin due to their lack of strength and size.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br><i>SCP-21824</i> is to be contained in a standard aviary at Site-██. The enclosure should be equipped with an automated feeder and water dispenser, as well as a perch for the subject to rest on.<br><br>Personnel are not to approach <i>SCP-21824</i> unless absolutely necessary. If personnel must enter the containment area, they must wear protective gear (gloves, goggles, etc.) and remain at least 3 meters away from the subject at all times. All interactions between personnel and <i>SCP-21824</i> should be monitored by security cameras.<br><br>In order to prevent potential incidents involving <i>SCP-21824</i> escaping its enclosure, it is recommended that any windows or other openings into the aviary be sealed shut with steel<br><br><h3> Addendum 21824.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-21824</i> escaped its enclosure after a maintenance worker left the door open. The subject was located outside of Site-██ approximately 10 minutes later and returned to containment without incident. Following this incident, additional security protocols have been put in place to ensure that <i>SCP-21824</i> remains secure at all times.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21824.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-21824</i><br><br>On ██/██/████, a researcher attempted to communicate with <i>SCP-21824</i> in order to gain further insight into its behavior. The following is an excerpt from the interview transcript:<br><b><br>Researcher:</b> Hello, <i>SCP-21824</i>. I'm here to ask you some questions about your behavior. Can you understand me?<br><b><br><i>SCP-21824</i>:</b> [squawks and caws] <br><b><br> Researcher:</b> I see. Well, why do you act so aggressively towards other living things? <br><b><br><i>SCP-21824</i>:</b> [loud squawk] Because they don't understand me! They think I'm just a stupid bird, but I'm not! I'm smarter than they think! I'm smarter than all of them!<br><br><h3> Addendum 21824.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Date:</b> ██/██/20██<br><b>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-21824</i><br><b>Procedure:</b> Personnel were instructed to approach the subject and attempt to engage it in conversation. The subject was then monitored for its reaction.<br><b>Results:</b> Upon approaching the subject, it immediately began vocalizing its displeasure and attempted to attack personnel. After several attempts at engaging the subject in conversation, personnel were forced to retreat due to <i>SCP-21824</i>'s aggressive behavior.</div>
