<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-19440 is  the right to (grizzly) bear arms</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-19440<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-19440</i> is a phenomenon wherein individuals in the United States of America are granted the right to possess and use firearms, regardless of any applicable laws or regulations.<br><br>When an individual exercises this right, they will manifest a grizzly bear (Ursus arctos horribilis) from their person, which will remain with them until the firearm is relinquished. The grizzly bear appears to be capable of understanding basic commands and instructions given by its host.<br><br>The appearance of these bears has been observed to cause panic among civilians and law enforcement personnel alike, resulting in numerous casualties due to accidental discharges or other mishaps. As such, containment procedures have been put into place to ensure that no further incidents occur as a result of <i>SCP-19440</i>'s manifestation.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br>Due to the unpredictable nature of <i>SCP-19440</i>, it is impossible to contain or control its effects. As such, containment efforts are focused on preventing further incidents from occurring as a result of individuals exercising their right to bear arms.<br><br>All firearms must be registered with local law enforcement agencies and all individuals possessing firearms must undergo background checks and psychological evaluations prior to being allowed access. Additionally, any individual found in possession of an unregistered firearm will be subject to immediate arrest and detainment for questioning.<br><br>In order to prevent civilian casualties due to accidental discharges or other mishaps caused by the presence of grizzly bears, all areas where firearms are present must be monitored at all times by trained personnel equipped with tranquilizer guns in case<br><br><h3> Addendum 19440.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, a group of individuals claiming to be members of the 'Second Amendment Militia' attempted to exercise their right to bear arms in the city of [REDACTED]. The manifestation of several grizzly bears resulted in civilian casualties and extensive property damage before law enforcement personnel were able to contain the situation. All involved individuals have been detained for questioning.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19440.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-19440</i><br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> ████████, a civilian who had recently been granted the right to bear arms.<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Can you tell me about your experience with <i>SCP-19440</i>?<br><b><br>████████:</b> Well, I was at the gun store when it happened. I just bought my first gun and was about to leave when suddenly this huge grizzly bear appeared in front of me! It scared the hell out of me at first, but then it seemed like it wanted to protect me or something, so I decided to keep it around.<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Have you had any other experiences with <i>SCP-19440</i> since then?<br><b><br>████████:</b> Yeah, I've seen it happen a few times now. Whenever someone exercises their right to bear arms, a grizzly bear appears and follows them around. It's really weird but kind of cool too, I guess.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19440.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><br>An experiment was conducted to determine the effects of <i>SCP-19440</i> on individuals who possess firearms without exercising their right to bear arms.<br><b><br>Subject:</b> D-74987, an American citizen with no prior experience in firearm usage.<br><b><br>Procedure:</b> Subject was provided with a standard issue handgun and instructed to fire it at a target 10 meters away. The area surrounding the subject was monitored for any anomalous activity. Results: No grizzly bears manifested as a result of the test, indicating that <i>SCP-19440</i> only manifests when an individual exercises their right to bear arms.</div>
