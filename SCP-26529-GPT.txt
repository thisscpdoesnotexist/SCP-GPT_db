    <h3>Item #:</h3>
<p>SCP-26529</p>
<h3>Object Class:</h3>
<p>Keter</p>
<h3>Description:</h3>
<p>SCP-26529 refers to an anomalous event that occurred in a parallel universe, henceforth designated as Universe-26529-A. This event was a gender reveal party that spiraled out of control due to a series of highly improbable and contrived circumstances. The cumulative effects of these circumstances resulted in the total annihilation of Universe-26529-A.</p>
<p>The exact nature of SCP-26529 is still under intensive investigation, but preliminary findings suggest that the gender reveal mechanism involved a complex interplay of reality-altering quantum devices, high-energy materials far exceeding any known explosives in our universe, and an as-yet-unknown ritual component that caused a cascading chain reaction across the fabric of Universe-26529-A.</p>
<p>Observations of SCP-26529 have been made possible through a temporal-spatial viewing apparatus which allows Foundation researchers to collect data without direct interaction or interference. It has been theorized that if such anomalous components exist or are created within our universe, they could pose a catastrophic risk and thus SCP-26529 has been classified as Keter.</p>
<h3>Special Containment Procedures:</h3>
<p>As SCP-26529 resulted in the destruction of Universe-26529-A, no direct containment is possible. Indirect containment efforts are focused on preventing the replication of such an event in our universe. Foundation efforts must include the following:</p>
<ol>
<li>Monitoring of all research in quantum physics and high-energy materials to detect any developments that could lead to SCP-26529-like events.</li>
<li>Infiltration and surveillance of groups that engage in ritualistic practices believed to have reality-altering potential.</li>
<li>Immediate interception and neutralization of any entities or devices capable of reality manipulation detected entering or created within our universe.</li>
<li>Deployment of misinformation campaigns to discredit and discourage elaborately dangerous gender reveal parties.</li>
<li>Maintenance and operation of viewing apparatuses to monitor for any aftershocks or related incidents within the remnants of Universe-26529-A.</li>
</ol>
<p>In the event that any phenomena resembling SCP-26529 are detected, Mobile Task Forces specialized in extradimensional threats are to be dispatched immediately to enact Protocol Nexus-Cascade, aiming to avert the destruction of our reality.</p>
