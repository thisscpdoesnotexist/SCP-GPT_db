<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-21019 is  the dungeon of fear and hunger</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-21019<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-21019</i> is a dungeon located in a remote area of the United States. The interior of <i>SCP-21019</i> consists of several rooms, each connected via narrow corridors and stairways. Each room contains various objects, including furniture, weapons, and items that appear to be foodstuffs.<br><br>The most notable feature of <i>SCP-21019</i> is its anomalous effect on those who enter it; all individuals within the dungeon become afflicted with an intense fear and hunger. These feelings are so strong that they cause physical symptoms such as nausea, dizziness, and trembling. Individuals affected by this phenomenon will attempt to flee from the dungeon as quickly as possible or else risk becoming overwhelmed by their fear and hunger until they collapse from exhaustion or starvation.<br><br>In addition to its anomalous effects on those inside it, <i>SCP-21019</i> also appears to have some form of sentience; it has been observed rearranging itself when left unattended for extended periods of time. It is unknown if this behavior is intentional or merely a result of its anomalous properties.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-21019</i> is to be contained in a secure facility at all times. Access to the dungeon is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher. Any individuals entering <i>SCP-21019</i> must be monitored for signs of distress and removed from the dungeon as soon as possible.<br><br>Any items found within <i>SCP-21019</i> are to be catalogued and stored in a secure location for further study.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21019.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, a team of researchers entered <i>SCP-21019</i> to investigate its anomalous properties. After several hours of exploration, the team encountered a humanoid entity in one of the rooms. The entity was approximately 1.5 meters tall and had pale skin and long black hair. It spoke English with an American accent and identified itself as <i>"the Fear Eater"</i>.<br><br>The Fear Eater explained that it resided within <i>SCP-21019</i> to feed on the fear and hunger generated by those who enter it, stating that it was drawn to such strong emotions like a moth to flame. When asked why it chose this particular dungeon, the Fear Eater replied that it felt comfortable within its walls due to its familiarity with them from previous visits over many years.<br><br>The Fear Eater offered no further information before disappearing shortly thereafter; however, researchers believe that this entity may be responsible for <i>SCP-21019</i>'s anomalous effects on those who enter it.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21019.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-21019</i><br><br>On ██/██/20██, a team of researchers entered <i>SCP-21019</i> in order to conduct an interview with it. The following is a transcript of the conversation that took place:<br><b><br>Researcher:</b> <i>"Hello, <i>SCP-21019</i>. We are here to ask you some questions."</i><br><b><br><i>SCP-21019</i>:</b> <i>"What do you wish to know?"</i><br><b><br>Researcher:</b> <i>"What is the purpose of this dungeon?"</i><br><b><br><i>SCP-21019</i>:</b> <i>"I exist to bring fear and hunger to those who enter. It is my nature, and I cannot be changed."</i><br><br><h3> Addendum 21019.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log 21019.3-1:</b> On ██/██/████, a D-Class personnel was instructed to enter <i>SCP-21019</i> and explore its interior for 10 minutes. After 8 minutes, the D-Class became overwhelmed by fear and hunger and attempted to flee the dungeon. The experiment was terminated after 9 minutes when the D-Class collapsed from exhaustion.<br><b><br>Experiment Log 21019.3-2:</b> On ██/██/████, two D-Class personnel were instructed to enter <i>SCP-21019</i> together with instructions to stay in contact with each other at all times. After 5 minutes, one of the individuals began exhibiting signs of distress and attempted to flee the dungeon; however, their companion managed to restrain them until they calmed down enough for both individuals to safely exit <i>SCP-21019</i>.</div>
