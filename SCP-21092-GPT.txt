<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-21092 is  an orange suit what manifests someone says "tangerine" 1000 times between 7 and 8 PM.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-21092<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-21092</i> is an orange suit of clothing, consisting of a jacket, trousers, and a tie. It was found in the back room of a thrift store in ██████, California.<br><br>When someone says the word <i>"tangerine"</i> within earshot of <i>SCP-21092</i> between 7 PM and 8 PM local time, it will animate itself and begin to move around as if it were being worn by an unseen person. The movements are limited to walking and gesturing with its arms; no facial features have been observed on the suit.<br><br>The suit will remain animated until either 8 PM passes or someone says <i>"tangerine"</i> 1000 times within that hour; once this occurs, <i>SCP-21092</i> will deactivate and return to its inert state until 7 PM rolls around again. If any other words are spoken during this period besides <i>"tangerine"</i>, they will be ignored by the suit's animation.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-21092</i> is to be kept in a standard containment locker at Site-██. The room containing the locker must be monitored by video surveillance at all times, and any personnel entering the room must wear noise cancelling headphones.<br><br>In order to prevent accidental activation of <i>SCP-21092</i>, no personnel are allowed to say <i>"tangerine"</i> within earshot of the suit between 7 PM and 8 PM local time. Any personnel found doing so will be reprimanded accordingly.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21092.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, a Foundation agent embedded in the local police department reported that a man had been arrested for an attempted robbery at the thrift store where <i>SCP-21092</i> was found. The man, identified as █████ ██████████, claimed to have purchased the suit from another customer at the store and believed it to be cursed. He stated that he had worn it several times before realizing its anomalous properties and attempting to sell it on the black market.<br><br><b>Interview with Mr. ██████████:</b><br><br><h3> Addendum 21092.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-21092</i><br><br>On ██/██/20██, an interview was conducted with <i>SCP-21092</i>. The following is a transcript of the conversation:<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Hello, <i>SCP-21092</i>. Can you hear me?<br><b><br><i>SCP-21092</i>:</b> [No response]<br><b><br> Interviewer:</b> Are you able to understand me?<br><b><br> <i>SCP-21092</i>:</b> Yes.<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Can you tell me why you animate when someone says <i>"tangerine"</i>?<br><b><br><i>SCP-21092</i>:</b> It is a game. I enjoy it.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21092.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Test 1:</b> A D-Class personnel was instructed to say <i>"tangerine"</i> 1000 times between 7 PM and 8 PM. The suit animated as expected, with no anomalous behavior observed. After the 1000th repetition of the word, <i>SCP-21092</i> deactivated and returned to its inert state.<br><b><br>Test 2:</b> A D-Class personnel was instructed to say <i>"orange"</i> 1000 times between 7 PM and 8 PM. No anomalous behavior was observed from <i>SCP-21092</i>; it remained in its inert state throughout the experiment.</div>
