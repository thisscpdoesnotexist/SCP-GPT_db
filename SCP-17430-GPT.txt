<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17430 is  a red cape that sets your Wanted level to max, but in real life.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17430<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17430</i> is a red cape made of silk and wool, with an unknown symbol sewn onto the back. When worn by any living creature, it will instantly increase their wanted level to maximum in any country they are currently present in. This effect appears to be permanent until the wearer removes <i>SCP-17430</i> from their body or travels outside of the affected country's borders.<br><br>The symbol on <i>SCP-17430</i>'s back has been identified as belonging to a small cult known as <i>"The Red Cloak Society"</i>, who worship an entity referred to only as <i>"The Great One"</i>. The group is believed to have originated in the early 19th century and was active until its apparent dissolution in 1892. It is unknown what connection this group has with <i>SCP-17430</i>, if any.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17430</i> is to be kept in a secure locker at Site-██. Access to <i>SCP-17430</i> is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher. Any personnel found wearing <i>SCP-17430</i> must be immediately detained and the item removed from their possession.<br><br>In the event of an attempted theft, all available security measures are to be taken in order to prevent the item from leaving Foundation custody.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17430.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, an instance of <i>SCP-17430</i> was recovered from a small village in [REDACTED], after reports of a man wearing the cape and running from police. The individual was apprehended and taken into custody by Foundation personnel.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17430.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-17430</i><br><b><br><b>Interviewed: </b> <i>SCP-17430</i><br><b><br></b> <b>Interviewer: </b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br></b> <br> Foreword:</b> Interview was conducted in order to gain a better understanding of <i>SCP-17430</i> and its effects.<br><br> &ltBegin Log&gt <br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> How did you come into existence? <br><b><i>SCP-17430</i>:</b> I do not know. I have been here for as long as I can remember, but it is impossible to say how or why I came to be here. <br><b>Dr. ██████:</b> What is the purpose of your effect? <br><b><i>SCP-17430</i>:</b> My purpose is simple; to ensure that those who wear me are never forgotten by the authorities, no matter how hard they try to hide. <br><b>Dr. ██████:</b> Do you have any other effects? <br><b><i>SCP-17430</i>:</b> No, my effect is limited to increasing the wearer's wanted level. <br><b>Dr. ██████:</b> Is there anything else you can tell us about yourself? <br><b><i>SCP-17430</i>:</b> The symbol on my back belongs to a cult known as <i>"The Red Cloak Society"</i>. I do not know what connection this group has with me, if any.  <br> &ltEnd Log&gt <br><b><br>Closing Statement:</b> Interview concluded. <i>SCP-17430</i> remains in containment.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17430.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Test #:</b> 17430-A<br><b><br>Subject:</b> D-93785, a male human of no particular note.<br><b><br>Procedure:</b> Subject was asked to put on <i>SCP-17430</i> and remain in the area for 5 minutes.<br><b><br>Results:</b> Immediately upon putting on <i>SCP-17430</i>, D-93785's wanted level increased to maximum in all countries within a 200km radius of Site ██. The effect persisted until the subject removed <i>SCP-17430</i> from his body and left the area.</div>
