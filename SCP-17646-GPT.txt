<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17646 is  a penguin-gorilla hybrid animal</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17646<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17646</i> is a hybrid animal composed of the physical characteristics of a penguin and gorilla. It has a black and white fur coat, with the head, wings, feet, and tail resembling those of a penguin. The body is more similar to that of a gorilla, with two arms and two legs ending in opposable thumbs. <i>SCP-17646</i> stands at 1 meter tall when fully upright.<br><br><i>SCP-17646</i>'s behavior is highly unpredictable; it can be docile or aggressive depending on its moods. It appears to have an affinity for water, often spending long periods swimming in any available pool or lake nearby its containment area. When not swimming or eating it will wander around aimlessly or sleep for extended periods of time.<br><br>Although <i>SCP-17646</i> does not appear to understand human language it does respond positively to gestures such as pointing and clapping; this may indicate some level of intelligence beyond that expected from an animal hybrid species.<br><br>Due to its unpredictable nature <i>SCP-17646</i> must be contained within an enclosure fitted with reinforced walls capable of containing both land animals and aquatic creatures simultaneously; personnel are advised to exercise extreme caution when interacting with the entity due to its potential aggression towards humans if provoked<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17646</i> is to be contained in a reinforced enclosure at Site-██. The enclosure must have both land and aquatic sections, with the water section being large enough for <i>SCP-17646</i> to swim comfortably.<br><br>Personnel are not allowed to enter the containment area without permission from Level 3 personnel or higher. Any interaction with <i>SCP-17646</i> must be done under direct supervision of a Level 3 personnel or higher.<br><br>In the event that <i>SCP-17646</i> displays signs of aggression, personnel are instructed to use nonlethal force if possible; lethal force is only authorized as a last resort in order to protect personnel and other living creatures within the vicinity.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17646.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/████, <i>SCP-17646</i> was observed attempting to construct a crude shelter using sticks and rocks. Upon further investigation it was discovered that the entity had been collecting materials from outside its enclosure and stockpiling them in its shelter. It is currently unknown how the entity managed to acquire these items without being detected by personnel.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17646.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-17646</i><br><b><br><b>Interviewed: </b> <i>SCP-17646</i> </b> <b>Interviewer:  Dr. ████████ </b> <br> Foreword: An attempt was made to communicate with <i>SCP-17646</i> using a combination of sign language and simple words in an effort to gain further insight into its behavior. &ltBegin Log&gt Dr. ████████: [Points at <i>SCP-17646</i>] Who are you? [<i>SCP-17646</i> responds by pointing at itself, then making a motion as if it is swimming.] Dr. ████████: Are you from the sea? [<i>SCP-17646</i> shakes its head no.] Dr. ████████: Are you from the land? [<i>SCP-17646</i> nods yes.] Dr. ████████: Do you like it here? [<i>SCP-17646</i> shrugs.] &ltEnd Log&gt Closing Statement: Further attempts to communicate with <i>SCP-17646</i> have been unsuccessful.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17646.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><br>Experiment Log 17646.3<br><b><br>Date:</b> ██/██/20██<br><b><br>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-17646</i><br><b><br>Procedure:</b> A live fish was placed in the aquatic section of <i>SCP-17646</i>'s containment enclosure. The subject was observed for any changes in behavior or attitude towards the fish. <br><b><br>Results:</b> Upon noticing the presence of the fish, <i>SCP-17646</i> became agitated and began to circle it aggressively. After several minutes, it lunged at the fish and swallowed it whole before returning to its normal activities.</div>
