<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18277 is  platform shoes that never end</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18277<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18277</i> is a pair of platform shoes, made from black leather. The shoes are designed to never end, and will always extend further than the user's feet.<br><br>When <i>SCP-18277</i> is worn by an individual, their feet will appear to be walking on air as they move forward. The shoes will continue to extend as long as the wearer continues moving in any direction; however, when the wearer stops or attempts to turn around, they will immediately be transported back to their starting point.<br><br>The maximum distance that can be traveled while wearing <i>SCP-18277</i> is unknown; however, it has been determined that if an individual wears them for more than 24 hours straight without rest or sleep, they would eventually reach a destination somewhere in outer space.<br><br>Attempts at removing <i>SCP-18277</i> from its wearer have been unsuccessful; once placed on someone's feet it cannot be removed until either 24 hours have passed or until the person reaches their destination (whichever comes first).<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18277</i> is to be kept in a secure locker at Site-██. Access to <i>SCP-18277</i> is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any personnel found wearing <i>SCP-18277</i> must be monitored closely and provided with food, water, and rest as needed. Personnel found attempting to use <i>SCP-18277</i> for unauthorized purposes will be reprimanded accordingly.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18277.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, a research team attempted to use <i>SCP-18277</i> to explore outer space. After 24 hours of continuous travel, the team returned with several samples of unknown extraterrestrial materials. Analysis of these samples is ongoing.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18277.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-18277</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> <i>SCP-18277</i><br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> What can you tell me about yourself?<br><b><br><i>SCP-18277</i>:</b> I am a pair of platform shoes that never end. I was created to help people explore the world and beyond, without ever having to worry about running out of ground beneath their feet.<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> How do you work?<br><b><br><i>SCP-18277</i>:</b> When someone wears me, I will extend infinitely as they move forward, and take them to places they have never seen before. However, when they stop or attempt to turn around, I will immediately transport them back to their starting point.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18277.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment 18277.1:</b> On ██/██/20██, D-Class personnel were instructed to wear <i>SCP-18277</i> and walk in a straight line for 24 hours. After the 24 hours had passed, the subject was found to have reached a destination approximately ███ kilometers away from their starting point.<br><b><br>Experiment 18277.2:</b> On ██/██/20██, D-Class personnel were instructed to wear <i>SCP-18277</i> and attempt to turn around while walking in a straight line for 24 hours. The subject was immediately transported back to their starting point after attempting to turn around, regardless of how far they had walked beforehand.</div>
