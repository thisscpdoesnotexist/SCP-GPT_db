(Warning: may cause xk-clasd end of world scenario)</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-21491<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-21491</i> is a large, black duck of unknown origin. Its physical features and behavior are consistent with that of a common mallard, however its eyes glow an eerie red color and it emits a low growling sound when agitated.<br><br>When in the presence of humans or other animals, <i>SCP-21491</i> will exhibit extreme aggression and hostility, attacking anything that comes into contact with it. It has been observed to be capable of flight at speeds far exceeding those of any known species of bird.<br><br>In addition to its aggressive behavior, <i>SCP-21491</i> also exhibits several anomalous properties which make it extremely dangerous. These include:<br><br>• The ability to manipulate reality on a small scale; allowing for the creation and manipulation of objects or creatures out of thin air as well as the alteration or destruction existing objects and creatures within its vicinity. <br>• An aura which causes all living beings in close proximity to experience intense fear or dread upon contact with <i>SCP-21491</i>; often resulting in panic attacks or even death by cardiac arrest if exposed for too long. <br>• A powerful telepathic link between itself and any human being who makes eye contact with it; this allows for complete control over their thoughts and actions while under the influence of this link. <br><br> As such, containment protocols have been put in place to ensure that no further damage is caused by <i>SCP-21491</i>'s actions should containment breach occur at any point during its tenure at Site ██ .<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-21491</i> is to be kept in a reinforced steel containment chamber at all times. The chamber must have no windows or other openings that could allow for the entry of any living creature.<br><br>The chamber must also be equipped with an automated security system which will activate if any unauthorized personnel attempt to enter the containment area. This system will include motion sensors, cameras, and an alarm system which will alert on-site personnel of any breach attempts. <br><br>In addition, two armed guards are to remain stationed outside of the containment area at all times in order to prevent any potential breaches from occurring. <br><br>Any personnel attempting to make contact with <i>SCP-21491</i> are required to wear protective gear and follow strict safety protocols while doing so;<br><br><h3> Addendum 21491.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-21491</i> escaped containment and flew into the nearby town of [REDACTED], causing extensive property damage and several casualties in the process. After a brief search, <i>SCP-21491</i> was eventually located and recaptured by MTF agents without incident. <br><br>In light of this event, security protocols have been further tightened to prevent any future incidents from occurring.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21491.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-21491</i><br><br>On ██/██/████, personnel at Site ██ conducted an interview with <i>SCP-21491</i> in order to gain a better understanding of its motives and goals. The following is a transcript of the interview: <br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Hello, <i>SCP-21491</i>. Can you tell us why you are here? <br><b><br><i>SCP-21491</i>:</b> I am here to bring about the end of all life on this planet. It is my mission to ensure that no living creature will survive when I have finished my work. <br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> What is your goal? <br><b><br><i>SCP-21491</i>:</b> My goal is to cause as much destruction and chaos as possible. I will do whatever it takes to achieve my mission, no matter the cost. <br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Do you have any remorse for the suffering you are causing? <br><b><br><i>SCP-21491</i>:</b> No. I do not feel any remorse for my actions. I am merely fulfilling my purpose and doing what must be done in order to achieve my goal.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21491.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log 21491.3:</b> <br><b>Subject:</b> D-Class personnel<br><b>Procedure:</b> D-class personnel was instructed to enter the containment chamber and make physical contact with <i>SCP-21491</i>. <br><b>Results:</b> Upon entering the containment chamber, D-class personnel immediately began to experience intense fear and panic. After making physical contact with <i>SCP-21491</i>, the subject went into cardiac arrest and died shortly afterwards.</div>
