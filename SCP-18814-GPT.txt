<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18814 is  the demon core. Not simply a spherical mass of subcritical plutonium, it is also a pre-babylonian satanic relic.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18814<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18814</i> is a spherical mass of subcritical plutonium, approximately 10 cm in diameter. It is believed to be the core of a pre-Babylonian demonic entity, and has been determined to possess an anomalous form of energy that can cause significant physical and mental harm when exposed to living organisms.<br><br>When exposed to any living organism, <i>SCP-18814</i> will cause intense feelings of dread and fear in the subject; this effect increases with proximity. In addition, it is capable of manipulating its environment through unknown means; for example, it has been observed causing objects within its vicinity to move or vanish entirely.<br><br><i>SCP-18814</i> also possesses limited telepathic abilities; it can communicate with those nearby as well as influence their thoughts and emotions. It appears that these effects are strongest when the subject is in close proximity or direct contact with <i>SCP-18814</i> itself. Furthermore, prolonged exposure may lead to permanent psychological damage or even death in extreme cases.<br><br>Due to its potential for destruction if left unchecked, <i>SCP-18814</i> has been classified as Keter level threat by the Foundation's Ethics Committee<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-18814</i> is to be contained in a secure containment chamber at Site-██. The chamber should be monitored by personnel wearing protective gear, and all personnel must maintain a minimum distance of 10 meters from the object at all times.<br><br>In addition, no living organism should ever come into contact with <i>SCP-18814</i>; if this occurs, the affected individual must immediately be removed from the area and given appropriate medical treatment. Any personnel exhibiting signs of psychological distress after coming into contact with <i>SCP-18814</i> are to receive immediate psychiatric evaluation and treatment as necessary.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18814.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/████, a containment breach occurred at Site-██. During the incident, <i>SCP-18814</i> was released and began to move freely throughout the facility. Despite attempts by personnel to contain it, <i>SCP-18814</i> managed to escape and has since not been located. All personnel affected by its presence during the incident have received appropriate medical treatment and psychological counseling as needed.<br><br><h3> Addendum 18814.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-18814</i><br><br>On ██/██/20██, an interview with <i>SCP-18814</i> was conducted by Dr. ██████.
