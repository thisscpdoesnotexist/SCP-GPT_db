<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-19408 is  an anarchist, transhumanist, economically center, culturally centrist ideology, inhabiting the bottom of the Political Compass. He advocates for transhumanism to expand the frontier into space.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-19408<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-19408</i> is an anomalous ideology, which appears to be a combination of anarchist and transhumanist beliefs. It has been observed that those who adhere to this ideology have a strong belief in the idea of “economic centrism”, where economic decisions are based on the common good rather than the interests of any particular group or individual.<br><br>Culturally speaking, <i>SCP-19408</i> advocates for a form of cultural centrism, where all cultures are respected and celebrated equally. This includes both traditional cultures as well as new technologies and ideas.<br><br>The most notable aspect of <i>SCP-19408</i> is its advocacy for transhumanism; specifically, it encourages humanity to expand out into space through technological advancement and exploration. In addition, it also encourages humans to use technology responsibly in order to improve their lives without damaging the environment or other species living on Earth.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> As <i>SCP-19408</i> is an ideology, it cannot be contained in the traditional sense. However, Foundation personnel are to monitor any individuals or groups that appear to adhere to this ideology and take appropriate action if necessary.<br><br>In addition, Foundation personnel are to monitor any media outlets for mentions of <i>SCP-19408</i> and its associated beliefs. Any such mentions should be reported immediately so that further investigation can take place.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19408.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, a group of individuals claiming to adhere to <i>SCP-19408</i> began protesting outside of the United Nations headquarters in New York City. The protesters were demanding that the UN recognize their ideology and take steps to ensure its spread throughout the world.<br><br>The protest was eventually dispersed by Foundation personnel and all participants were detained for questioning. No further incidents have been reported since then.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19408.2: </h3> Interview with an adherent<br><br>On ██/██/████, Foundation personnel conducted a brief interview with an individual who claimed to adhere to <i>SCP-19408</i>. It was determined that the individual had no knowledge of the anomalous nature of their beliefs. The following is a transcript of the interview:<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Could you tell us about your beliefs?<br><b><br>Adherent:</b> Sure! I believe in economic centrism and cultural centrism, which means that all cultures should be respected and celebrated equally. I also believe in transhumanism; we should use technology responsibly to improve our lives without damaging the environment or other species living on Earth. Finally, I think humanity should expand out into space through technological advancement and exploration.<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> What do you think about the current state of the world?<br><b><br>Adherent:</b> I think it's a mess. We're stuck in this cycle of greed and inequality, where only the wealthy benefit from our economic system. We need to break out of this cycle and find a way to make sure everyone has access to resources and opportunities. I also believe that we should be more responsible with our technology; too often, we use it irresponsibly without considering the consequences.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19408.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><br>On ██/██/████, an experiment was conducted to observe the effects of <i>SCP-19408</i> on individuals. Three D-Class personnel were exposed to a recording of an individual espousing <i>SCP-19408</i>’s beliefs. After exposure, all three subjects reported feeling more optimistic about their lives and expressed a desire to explore space. All three subjects also reported feeling more connected with others and had a newfound appreciation for different cultures and ideas.</div>
