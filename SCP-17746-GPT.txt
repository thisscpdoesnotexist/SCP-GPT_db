Its surface is smooth and featureless except for two small protrusions on the top and bottom which emit a bright yellow light. The object emits a low humming noise when active.<br><br>When an organic life form comes within 5 meters of <i>SCP-17746</i>, it will become active and begin emitting a series of high pitched noises at varying frequencies. These noises appear to be directed towards the individual in question, as if attempting to communicate with them. At this point, any attempts to interact with <i>SCP-17746</i> have been unsuccessful.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17746</i> is to be contained in a standard containment chamber at Site-██.
