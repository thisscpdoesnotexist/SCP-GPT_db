<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17534 is  lamia snake woman</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17534<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17534</i> is a humanoid entity resembling a lamia, or half-woman, half-snake creature. <i>SCP-17534</i> stands at approximately 1.8 meters in height and possesses a long tail with scales similar to those of a snake. Its head is that of an adult female human with green eyes and black hair which extends down its back and onto the tip of its tail.<br><br><i>SCP-17534</i>'s primary anomalous effect manifests when it comes into contact with any living being other than itself; upon contact, <i>SCP-17534</i> will begin to drain the life force from them in order to sustain itself, resulting in death within minutes if not stopped by other means. Furthermore, after draining the life force from an individual <i>SCP-17534</i> will gain temporary enhanced physical capabilities such as increased strength and speed for up to 24 hours before returning to normal levels.<br><br>In addition, <i>SCP-17534</i> has demonstrated limited control over snakes within 10 meters of its location; these snakes are able to be controlled by mental commands given by <i>SCP-17534</i> and can be used as weapons against targets it perceives as hostile or threatening towards it or others nearby.<br><br>Due to the nature of its abilities and potential threat posed by them should they be used maliciously, containment procedures for <i>SCP-17534</i> have been classified as Keter level protocols involving constant monitoring using surveillance cameras located around Site ██ where it is currently contained along with two armed guards on duty at all times who are authorized lethal force if necessary should an escape attempt occur.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br><i>SCP-17534</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site ██. The cell should be equipped with surveillance cameras and two armed guards must be present at all times. Any personnel entering the containment chamber must wear protective gear and take caution when interacting with <i>SCP-17534</i> as it may attempt to drain their life force if given the opportunity.<br><br>In order to prevent any potential escape attempts, an electric fence has been erected around the perimeter of Site ██ and motion sensors have been installed within the facility itself which will alert security personnel if any unauthorized movement is detected.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17534.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-17534</i> was discovered in a small village in [REDACTED] after reports of multiple deaths due to unknown causes were reported.
