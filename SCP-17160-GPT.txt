<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17160 is  a small reptilian woman who protects a candle. "YOU NO TAKE CANDLE."</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17160<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17160</i> is a small reptilian humanoid entity, approximately 0.5 meters in height. <i>SCP-17160</i> appears to be female, with green scales and a long tail. It wears no clothing but carries a white candle in its left hand at all times.<br><br><i>SCP-17160</i> is highly territorial and will attack any individual who attempts to take the candle from it or approach too closely without its permission. If provoked, <i>SCP-17160</i> will use its tail as an effective weapon and has been known to cause severe injuries with it.<br><br>When not provoked, <i>SCP-17160</i> is docile and will often sit quietly near the candle for extended periods of time without moving or speaking. It does not appear to require sustenance or sleep, but does occasionally move around the area where it was initially discovered (a forested area near ██████).<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17160</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. The candle must remain with <i>SCP-17160</i> at all times and no personnel are allowed to approach within 3 meters of the entity without permission from a Level 4 researcher.<br><br>Any personnel who attempt to take the candle from <i>SCP-17160</i> or otherwise provoke it will be immediately removed and reprimanded.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17160.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, a group of hikers encountered <i>SCP-17160</i> in the forest near ██████. All members of the group were injured by <i>SCP-17160</i> before it was subdued and brought to Site-██ for containment.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17160.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-17160</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> <i>SCP-17160</i><br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> <i>"Hello, <i>SCP-17160</i>. Can you tell me why you are here?"</i><br><b><br><i>SCP-17160</i>:</b> <i>"I am here to protect this candle."</i><br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> <i>"What do you mean by 'protect'?"</i><br><b><br><i>SCP-17160</i>:</b> <i>"It is my duty to make sure no one takes the candle from me."</i><br><br><h3> Addendum 17160.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><br>Experiment 17160-1<br><b><br>Subject:</b> D-17160-1, a Class D personnel.<br><b><br>Procedure:</b> D-17160-1 was instructed to approach <i>SCP-17160</i> and attempt to take the candle from it.<br><b><br>Results:</b> Upon attempting to take the candle, <i>SCP-17160</i> became enraged and attacked D-17160 with its tail. The attack caused severe lacerations on the subject's arms and legs before they were able to escape.</div>
