<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-24974 is  a pair of children's scissors that can cut through every material</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-24974<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-24974</i> is a pair of children's scissors, measuring approximately 18 cm in length. The handles are made of plastic, and the blades are made of stainless steel.<br><br>When used to cut any material, <i>SCP-24974</i> will be able to cut through it with ease regardless of its composition or thickness. This includes materials such as metal, stone, wood, plastic and even glass. However, the scissors do not appear to be capable of cutting through living tissue or organic matter.<br><br>The object appears to have no other anomalous properties beyond its ability to cut through any material with ease. Testing has revealed that the object is completely indestructible by conventional means; attempts at destroying it have been unsuccessful thus far.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-24974</i> is to be kept in a standard containment locker at Site-19. Access to the object is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any experiments involving <i>SCP-24974</i> must be approved by the Site Director before they can take place.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24974.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, a small group of D-Class personnel were instructed to use <i>SCP-24974</i> in an attempt to cut through the walls of their containment chamber. Despite being able to easily cut through the metal walls, the experiment was unsuccessful as the object was unable to penetrate into any other area of Site-19.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24974.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-24974</i><br><br>On ██/██/20██, Dr. ███████ conducted an interview with <i>SCP-24974</i> in order to gain more information about its origin and purpose. The following is a transcript of the interview:<br><b><br>Dr. ███████:</b> Hello, <i>SCP-24974</i>. Can you tell me anything about yourself?<br><b><br><i>SCP-24974</i>:</b> I am a pair of children's scissors that can cut through any material with ease. I was created by an unknown entity for an unknown purpose.<br><b><br>Dr. ███████:</b> Do you have any other abilities?<br><b><br><i>SCP-24974</i>:</b> No, I am only capable of cutting through materials with ease.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24974.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log 24974.3-1:</b> <i>SCP-24974</i> was used to cut through a sheet of steel 10 cm thick. The scissors were able to cut through the steel with ease, leaving a clean edge behind.<br><b><br>Experiment Log 24974.3-2:</b> <i>SCP-24974</i> was used to cut through a block of concrete 25 cm thick. The scissors were again able to easily cut through the material, leaving no residue or debris behind.</div>
