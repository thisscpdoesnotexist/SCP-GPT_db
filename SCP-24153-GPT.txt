Subjects will become increasingly agitated and hostile if not provided with coffee quickly, and will attempt to acquire it by any means necessary.<br><br>Once the subject has consumed the coffee, their behavior returns to normal and they are capable of performing tasks as usual. However, subjects remain susceptible to <i>SCP-24153</i>'s effects should they be exposed again in the future.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-24153</i> is to be contained in a standard containment locker at Site-██. Access to the locker is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any personnel exposed to <i>SCP-24153</i> must be provided with coffee immediately, and monitored for any further effects of the anomaly. In addition, all personnel are required to maintain a minimum level of caffeine intake throughout their shifts in order to prevent further exposure.<br><br><h3> Addendum 24153.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, a research team was dispatched to investigate <i>SCP-24153</i>'s origin. During the investigation, it was discovered that the phenomenon originated from a small coffee shop located in [REDACTED], ██████.<br><br>The shop had been abandoned for several years prior to the investigation, and all records of its ownership have since been lost.
