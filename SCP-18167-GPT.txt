<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-18167 is  the slavic goddess of hangovers and consequences</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-18167<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-18167</i> is an incorporeal entity of Slavic origin, believed to be the goddess of hangovers and consequences. <i>SCP-18167</i> appears as a translucent humanoid female figure, standing at approximately 2 meters in height, with long black hair and pale skin.<br><br><i>SCP-18167</i> has the ability to manifest physical symptoms related to alcohol consumption in any living creature within its proximity. These symptoms include nausea, dizziness, headache and fatigue. In addition, <i>SCP-18167</i> can cause psychological effects such as memory loss and depression. The severity of these effects are determined by how much alcohol was consumed prior to encountering <i>SCP-18167</i>.<br><br>When provoked or threatened by any living creature within its vicinity, <i>SCP-18167</i> will retaliate by amplifying the physical and psychological effects it has caused on them exponentially until they either succumb to their ailments or flee from its presence. It is unknown if this effect has a limit or not; however all attempts so far have resulted in severe injury or death due to excessive intoxication levels beyond what would normally be considered safe for human consumption. <br><br><i>SCP-18167</i> appears able to move freely throughout populated areas without detection; however it seems that it prefers secluded locations where people often drink excessively such as bars or nightclubs due to the increased potential for victims there<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br>Due to the nature of <i>SCP-18167</i>, it is impossible to contain or capture. As such, containment efforts are focused on preventing public knowledge of its existence and mitigating any potential damage caused by its presence.<br><br>Personnel assigned to investigate reports of <i>SCP-18167</i>’s activity are required to wear protective gear and carry a supply of antidotes for alcohol poisoning in order to reduce the severity of symptoms should they become affected by <i>SCP-18167</i>’s abilities. In addition, personnel must be trained in basic self defense techniques as a precaution against possible retaliation from <i>SCP-18167</i> if provoked. <br><br>Any reports or sightings of <i>SCP-18167</i> must be reported immediately so that appropriate measures can be taken. The following is an excerpt from the interview transcript:<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> “What are your goals? What do you hope to achieve by punishing those who drink too much?” <br><b><br><i>SCP-18167</i>:</b> “My goal is simple; I want people to learn from their mistakes and think twice before they indulge in alcohol again. Too often people forget the consequences that come with drinking too much and I am here to remind them of that fact.”<br><br><h3> Addendum 18167.3: </h3> Experiment Logs<br><b><br>Experiment 1:</b><br><b><br>Subjects:</b> D-63714 and D-63715, both with a blood alcohol level of 0.08% <br><b><br>Procedure:</b> The two subjects were placed in a room together and instructed to drink alcohol until they reached an intoxication level of 0.20%. Once the desired level was achieved, <i>SCP-18167</i> was introduced into the room. <br><b><br> Results:</b> Both subjects began exhibiting symptoms associated with excessive alcohol consumption almost immediately after <i>SCP-18167</i>’s introduction; nausea, dizziness and headache being the most prominent effects. After approximately 5 minutes of exposure to <i>SCP-18167</i>’s presence, both subjects had lost consciousness due to their high levels of intoxication. <br><b><br> Conclusion:</b> Exposure to <i>SCP-18167</i> can cause severe physical and psychological effects on individuals who have consumed large amounts of alcohol prior to its arrival</div>
