<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-19893 is  ghost bustiers</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-19893<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-19893</i> is a set of four bustiers, each in a different color. When worn by any human female, the wearer will become invisible to all other humans. The invisibility effect only applies when the wearer remains stationary; any movement will cause them to become visible again.<br><br>The colors of the bustiers are pink, yellow, blue and green respectively. It appears that they were designed for use during Halloween parties or other similar events as they have been decorated with various spooky symbols such as skulls and bats.<br><br>It is unknown how <i>SCP-19893</i> works or where it originated from but it has been theorized that its effects may be related to some form of quantum entanglement phenomenon or an advanced form of optical camouflage technology.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-19893</i> is to be kept in a secure locker at Site-██. Access to the locker is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any personnel wishing to use <i>SCP-19893</i> must first submit a request form detailing their intended use of the object and receive approval from the site director before being allowed access. All testing involving <i>SCP-19893</i> must be supervised by at least two (2) security personnel and all test subjects must sign a waiver releasing any liability for any physical or psychological harm that may occur as a result of using the object.<br><br>Any individuals affected by <i>SCP-19893</i> are not permitted to leave the premises until they have been fully debriefed and cleared for release by medical staff.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19893.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, an incident occurred at Site-██ involving <i>SCP-19893</i>. A female D-Class personnel wearing the pink bustier managed to escape containment and was only apprehended after a search of the premises. After being returned to her cell, she was debriefed and reported that she had experienced a feeling of euphoria while invisible which made it difficult for her to remain still. She also stated that she felt compelled to explore the facility in order to find something but could not remember what it was.<br><br>Further investigation into this incident is ongoing.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19893.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-19893</i><br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> [REDACTED]<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████████<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> Can you tell me how this item works?<br><b><br>[REDACTED]:</b> It's pretty simple, really. When I put it on, I become invisible to everyone around me. It's like a cloak of invisibility!<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> Interesting. Do you know how it works?<br><b><br>[REDACTED]:</b> No, I don't. It's a mystery to me!<br><br><h3> Addendum 19893.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment #1:</b><br><b>Subject:</b> D-4563, female human. Procedure: Subject was instructed to put on the pink bustier and remain stationary for five (5) minutes. Results: After approximately three (3) minutes of remaining still, the subject became invisible to all observers in the room. The invisibility effect lasted for around two (2) minutes before fading away again. No other anomalous effects were observed during or after the experiment.</div>
