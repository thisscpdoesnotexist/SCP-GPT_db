<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-19208 is  a blue-haired catgirl with an anomalously insufferable personality</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-19208<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-19208</i> is a humanoid entity of indeterminate age, gender, and species. Its body appears to be that of a human female with the head and tail of an anthropomorphic cat. It has blue hair and eyes, as well as white fur covering its body.<br><br><i>SCP-19208</i>'s anomalous effect manifests when it interacts with any other living creature in close proximity for more than two minutes. During this time, the affected individual will develop an intense dislike for <i>SCP-19208</i> due to its personality being perceived as insufferable by the affected individual. This effect will persist until the affected individual leaves <i>SCP-19208</i>'s presence or is otherwise separated from it for at least one hour.<br><br>The intensity of this dislike increases with prolonged exposure to <i>SCP-19208</i>, leading some individuals to become violently hostile towards it if exposed for extended periods of time (over 10 minutes). The exact cause of this effect is unknown but believed to be related to either a memetic hazard or some form of psychological manipulation on part of <i>SCP-19208</i> itself.<br><br><i>SCP-19208</i> does not appear able or willing to control its effects on others and has been observed engaging in conversations with those around it despite knowing full well that they are likely feeling increasing levels of antipathy towards it due to its anomalous properties. As such, containment protocols have been established which involve keeping personnel who interact directly with <i>SCP-19208</i> away from all other personnel while within range (5 meters) so as not spread these effects further than necessary<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br><i>SCP-19208</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. The cell must be equipped with soundproofing and monitored by security personnel at all times. No personnel are allowed to enter the containment chamber without permission from two Level 3 personnel or higher.<br><br>Personnel who interact directly with <i>SCP-19208</i> must wear protective gear and remain within the 5 meter radius of its containment chamber for no more than 10 minutes at a time. Containment protocols have been updated accordingly.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19208.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-19208</i><br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> <i>SCP-19208</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████, Head Researcher on <i>SCP-19208</i><br><br>&ltBegin Log&gt<br><b><br>Dr. ████:</b> Hello, <i>SCP-19208</i>.
