<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-25103 is  a petrification meme</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-25103<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-25103</i> is a memetic entity that causes petrification in all living organisms it comes into contact with. The meme manifests as a series of images, videos and text which depict a humanoid figure composed entirely of stone. When exposed to <i>SCP-25103</i>, the affected organism will gradually become petrified over the course of several minutes before completely transforming into rock.<br><br>The effects of <i>SCP-25103</i> are not limited to physical transformation; psychological effects have also been observed in those affected by the meme. Subjects report feelings of dread and fear as their body begins to transform, followed by an overwhelming sense of peace and acceptance once they have fully transformed into stone.<br><br>The origin or purpose behind <i>SCP-25103</i> is currently unknown, though research suggests that it may be related to some form of ancient ritualistic practice or belief system involving petrification as a means of achieving immortality or spiritual ascension.<br><b><br>Containment Procedures:</b> Due to its pervasive nature and potential for widespread destruction, containment efforts for <i>SCP-25103</i> must focus on limiting its spread while researching possible methods for neutralization or prevention. All personnel assigned to work with this anomaly must be made aware that exposure can result in permanent physical transformation and take appropriate precautions when handling any materials associated with it. Additionally, any known instances should be monitored closely for signs of further spread beyond their initial point of origin.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-25103</i> is to be contained in a secure containment chamber at Site-██. The chamber should be equipped with an airlock system and monitored by security personnel at all times. All personnel entering the chamber must wear protective gear, including respirators, to prevent any potential exposure to the memetic entity.<br><br>Any instances of <i>SCP-25103</i> encountered outside of containment are to be reported immediately and neutralized as quickly as possible using standard memetic countermeasures.<br><br><h3> Addendum 25103.1: </h3> On ██/██/████, a containment breach occurred at Site-██ due to an unauthorized personnel entering the containment chamber. All personnel within the vicinity were exposed to <i>SCP-25103</i> and subsequently petrified. Containment protocols have since been updated in order to prevent any further breaches of this nature.<br><br><h3> Addendum 25103.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-25103</i><br><br>On ██/██/████, researchers at Site-██ conducted an interview with a petrified subject affected by <i>SCP-25103</i>. The following is a transcript of the conversation.<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Can you tell us what happened to you?<br><b><br>Subject:</b> I was exposed to the meme. It started as an image, and then it spread. Before I knew it, my body was turning to stone. I felt… peaceful, like I had been released from something.<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Do you know where the meme came from?<br><b><br>Subject:</b> No. All I know is that it was powerful, and it changed me in a way that I can never go back from.<br><br><h3> Addendum 25103.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><br>The following is a log of experiments conducted in an attempt to determine the nature and effects of <i>SCP-25103</i>.<br><b><br>Experiment 25103-1:</b> Two D-Class personnel were exposed to <i>SCP-25103</i> for five minutes before being removed from the chamber. Both subjects reported feeling a sense of dread as their bodies began to transform, followed by a sense of peace and acceptance once they had fully petrified. After one hour, both subjects returned to their original state with no lasting physical or psychological effects observed.</div>
