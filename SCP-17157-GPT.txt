<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17157 is  Literally just a US marine.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17157<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17157</i> is a human male, aged approximately 25 years old. <i>SCP-17157</i> was formerly a member of the United States Marine Corps, and has been designated as a Keter class object due to its anomalous properties.<br><br>When in close proximity to any living organism, <i>SCP-17157</i> will enter into an altered state of consciousness where it displays heightened levels of aggression and violence. During this state, <i>SCP-17157</i> will attempt to attack any living creature within range with extreme force and precision. In addition, it appears that <i>SCP-17157</i> possesses superhuman strength and speed which allow it to overpower most opponents with ease.<br><br>In order to prevent the spread of its anomalous effects, personnel assigned to containment must wear protective gear at all times when handling or interacting with <i>SCP-17157</i>. Furthermore, no personnel are allowed within five meters of the subject unless absolutely necessary for testing purposes or other approved activities by Level 4 personnel or higher.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17157</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment chamber at Site-██. The chamber must be equipped with reinforced walls, and any personnel entering the chamber must wear protective gear at all times.<br><br>In addition, two armed guards are to remain outside of the containment chamber at all times and are to be equipped with tranquilizer guns in order to subdue <i>SCP-17157</i> if necessary. All personnel assigned to <i>SCP-17157</i>'s containment must undergo psychological evaluations on a monthly basis.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17157.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-17157</i> was observed attempting to breach containment by smashing through the reinforced walls of its chamber. After a brief struggle, the subject was successfully subdued and contained. Following this incident, all personnel assigned to <i>SCP-17157</i>'s containment must be equipped with tranquilizer guns in order to subdue it if necessary.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17157.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-17157</i><br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> <i>SCP-17157</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████████, Site Director of Site-██.<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> Good morning, <i>SCP-17157</i>. Please state your name for the record.<br><b><br><i>SCP-17157</i>:</b> [The subject remains silent.]<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> Very well then, let's begin with some basic questions to get to know you better. How long have you been a member of the United States Marine Corps?<br><b><br><i>SCP-17157</i>:</b> I was in the Marines for about five years before I was discharged due to a medical condition that affected my ability to perform my duties properly.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17157.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Date:</b> ██/██/20██<br><b>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-17157</i><br><b>Procedure:</b> A test was conducted to determine the effects of <i>SCP-17157</i>'s anomalous properties on living organisms. Two D-class personnel were placed in a room with <i>SCP-17157</i>, and instructed to remain still for five minutes.<br><b>Results:</b> Upon entering the room, <i>SCP-17157</i> immediately entered into its altered state of consciousness and proceeded to attack both D-class personnel. Both personnel were killed within seconds due to the subject's overwhelming strength and speed.</div>
