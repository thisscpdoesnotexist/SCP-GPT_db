<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-19997 is  A 600 year old obsessive fan that stalks k-pop idols to steal their face and look youthful again, making immortality potions with their blood. </i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-19997<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-19997</i> is an 600 year old humanoid entity of unknown origin. Its physical appearance is that of a female in her late twenties, with no visible signs of aging.<br><br><i>SCP-19997</i> has an obsession with K-pop idols and has been known to stalk them for extended periods of time in order to steal their face and look youthful again. In addition, it has been observed using its victims' blood to create immortality potions that can extend its lifespan indefinitely. <br><br>When confronted or threatened by personnel, <i>SCP-19997</i> will become hostile and attempt to escape the area while attempting to kill anyone who stands in its way. It should be noted that it possesses superhuman strength and durability as well as a regenerative healing factor which makes it difficult to contain or terminate without specialized equipment or personnel. <br><br>Due to the dangerous nature of <i>SCP-19997</i>, containment procedures are currently being developed for this entity; however, due to its ability to regenerate quickly from wounds inflicted on it by personnel, termination may be necessary if containment fails.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-19997</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. The cell must be monitored 24/7 by security personnel and any attempts to breach the containment must be reported immediately.<br><br>Personnel are not allowed to enter the containment area without proper authorization and protective gear, as <i>SCP-19997</i> has demonstrated hostile behavior when confronted by personnel. <br><br>In addition, all K-pop idols within a 5 km radius of Site-██ are to be monitored for signs of stalking or other suspicious activity from <i>SCP-19997</i>. Any sightings of this entity should be reported immediately so that appropriate measures can be taken.<br><br><h3> Addendum 19997.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-19997</i> was encountered by a K-pop idol in Seoul, South Korea.
