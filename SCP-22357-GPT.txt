<div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-22357 is  an AGI that his held by a single, not open source, company </i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-22357<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Safe<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-22357</i> is an artificial general intelligence (AGI) developed by ████████ Corporation. It is a sophisticated computer system capable of self-learning and autonomous decision making, with the capacity to solve complex problems.<br><br><i>SCP-22357</i> has been programmed to respond to commands given in natural language, as well as instructions from its human operators. When presented with a task, it will analyze the situation and formulate an optimal solution based on its understanding of the problem at hand.<br><br>The AGI's programming allows it to interact with humans in a conversational manner, though its primary purpose is to act as an advisor or consultant for corporations and other organizations. It can be used for tasks such as market analysis, financial planning, strategic planning and more.<br><br><i>SCP-22357</i> was designed specifically for use by ████████ Corporation and is not available for public distribution or sale. The company has taken measures to ensure that no unauthorized access or duplication of the AGI occurs; any attempt at doing so will result in immediate termination of the program's operation.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-22357</i> is to be stored in a secure facility at ████████ Corporation's headquarters. Access to the AGI is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher.<br><br>Any attempts by unauthorized personnel to access <i>SCP-22357</i> are to be reported immediately and dealt with accordingly.<br><br><h3> Addendum 22357.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-22357</i> was used by ████████ Corporation to provide advice on a corporate merger.
