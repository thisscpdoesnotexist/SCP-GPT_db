<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-21245 is  my hot wrestler ex-wife, who took all my ladders in the divorce</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-21245<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Keter<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-21245</i> is a humanoid entity of indeterminate gender, approximately 1.2 meters in height. It appears to be composed entirely of ladders, with its body and limbs appearing to be made up of various types and sizes of ladders held together by an unknown adhesive substance.<br><br><i>SCP-21245</i> displays sapience and has the ability to communicate through a series of unintelligible noises and gestures. It is capable of manipulating objects with its ladder components, although the extent to which it can do so is currently unknown.<br><br>When provoked or threatened, <i>SCP-21245</i> will attempt to climb onto any nearby vertical surface using its ladder components, often resulting in significant property damage due to its weight and size. If unable to reach such surfaces, it will attempt to flee the area while continuing its attempts at climbing walls or other objects until it reaches safety or exhaustion sets in.<br><br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3><br><br><i>SCP-21245</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. The walls of the cell are to be lined with non-porous materials, and all windows and other openings must be sealed shut. All personnel entering the containment area must wear protective clothing and equipment suitable for dealing with ladder components.<br><br>The cell is to be monitored at all times by security cameras, and any attempts by <i>SCP-21245</i> to escape or cause harm are to be reported immediately. In addition, personnel should remain vigilant for any signs of unusual behavior from <i>SCP-21245</i> which may indicate an attempt at escaping its containment.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21245.1: </h3><br><br>On ██/██/20██, a Foundation investigation into reports of an unusual humanoid figure in ███████, ██ revealed <i>SCP-21245</i>. After being contained by Mobile Task Force Delta-9 (<i>"Ladder Runners"</i>), <i>SCP-21245</i> was brought to Site-██ for further study and containment.<br><br><h3> Addendum 21245.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-21245</i><br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> <i>SCP-21245</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ████████<br><b><br>Dr. ████████:</b> Good morning, <i>SCP-21245</i>. Can you tell me why you took all the ladders in the divorce?<br><b><br><i>SCP-21245</i>:</b> [No response]<br><br><h3> Addendum 21245.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Experiment Log 21245.1:</b><br><b><br>Subject:</b> <i>SCP-21245</i><br><b>Procedure:</b> A ladder was placed in the containment cell and <i>SCP-21245</i> was instructed to climb it. <br><b>Results:</b> <i>SCP-21245</i> successfully climbed the ladder, reaching the top within five minutes. Upon reaching the top, it remained there for approximately ten minutes before descending again and returning to its resting position on the floor of its containment cell.</div>
