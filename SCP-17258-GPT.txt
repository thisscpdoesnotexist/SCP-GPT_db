<style>.justifier {  text-align: justify;  text-justify: inter-word;}</style><div class='justifier'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17258 is  A handsome looking man with a nice voice, that can transform into a monster.</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17258<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Thaumiel<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17258</i> is a humanoid entity with the ability to transform into a monstrous form. In its humanoid form, <i>SCP-17258</i> appears as a handsome man of Caucasian descent, approximately 25 years old. He has short brown hair and blue eyes, and speaks in a pleasant voice.<br><br>When provoked or threatened, <i>SCP-17258</i> can transform into its monster form. This transformation takes about 10 seconds to complete and results in an enormous creature standing at least 8 meters tall with dark green skin covered in spikes and horns. Its face is distorted with large teeth protruding from its mouth and two glowing red eyes on either side of its head. It also possesses four tentacles that it uses for locomotion as well as combat purposes.<br><br>In this form, <i>SCP-17258</i> retains all the same abilities it had in human form but gains enhanced strength, speed, agility and durability; allowing it to easily overpower most opponents without suffering any significant damage itself. It also has access to various supernatural powers such as teleportation, energy projection (in the forms of fireballs or ice blasts) and telepathy amongst others; making it an incredibly powerful opponent when engaged in battle..<br><br><i>SCP-17258</i>'s primary purpose is to protect Foundation personnel from hostile entities; using both its physical prowess as well as its supernatural abilities if necessary. However due to the unpredictable nature of these powers they must be used under strict supervision by trained personnel only when absolutely necessary<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17258</i> is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-██. It is to be provided with basic amenities such as food, water and clothing.<br><br>Personnel are not permitted to enter <i>SCP-17258</i>'s containment chamber without permission from the site director or their appointed representative. If personnel must enter the chamber they must do so in groups of no less than three and always accompanied by at least one armed guard.<br><br>In the event that <i>SCP-17258</i> transforms into its monster form, all personnel are to evacuate the area immediately and contact security forces for assistance if necessary.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17258.1: </h3> Interview Log<br><b><br>Interviewed:</b> <i>SCP-17258</i><br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><br><h3> Addendum 17258.2: </h3> Interview with <i>SCP-17258</i><br><b><br><b>Interviewed: </b> <i>SCP-17258</i><br><b><br></b> <b>Interviewer: </b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br></b> <br> Foreword:</b> Interview was conducted after <i>SCP-17258</i> had been contained for two weeks.<br><br>&ltBegin Log&gt<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Good morning, <i>SCP-17258</i>.
