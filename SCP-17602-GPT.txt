<div class='justifytext'><center> <h3> <i>SCP-17602 is  the proverbial "chick magnet"</i> </h3> </center><h3> Item #: </h3> SCP-17602<br><br><h3> Object Class: </h3>Euclid<br><br><h3> Description: </h3> <i>SCP-17602</i> is an anomalous phenomenon that affects all males between the ages of 18 and 35. Any male within this age range who comes into contact with <i>SCP-17602</i> will experience a sudden increase in their attractiveness to female individuals, regardless of any physical or mental characteristics. This effect appears to be universal, as it has been observed in humans and non-humans alike.<br><br>When a subject is affected by <i>SCP-17602</i>, they will become the center of attention for any female individual within their vicinity. Females will become drawn to the subject and express strong feelings of attraction towards them, often leading to romantic relationships developing between them. The effects last for up to two weeks after initial exposure before fading away gradually over time.<br><br>The source of <i>SCP-17602</i>'s anomalous properties are unknown at this time; however, research is ongoing in order to determine its origin and potential uses or applications.<br><br><h3> Special Containment Procedures: </h3> <i>SCP-17602</i> is to be kept in a secure containment locker at Site-██. Access to <i>SCP-17602</i> is restricted to personnel with Level 3 clearance or higher. Any male personnel between the ages of 18 and 35 who come into contact with <i>SCP-17602</i> must be monitored for two weeks after exposure, and any romantic relationships that develop during this time must be reported immediately.<br><br>Any female individuals affected by <i>SCP-17602</i>'s effects are to be administered Class A amnestics as soon as possible in order to prevent further spread of its anomalous properties.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17602.1: </h3> On ██/██/20██, <i>SCP-17602</i> was discovered in the possession of a civilian male who had been reported missing by his family. The individual had been using <i>SCP-17602</i> to seduce women and form romantic relationships with them. After being contained, all affected individuals were administered Class A amnestics and released from containment.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17602.2: </h3> Interview with Subject<br><b><br>Interviewer:</b> Dr. ██████<br><b><br>Subject:</b> D-17602-1, a male human aged 25.<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Please state your name and age for the record.<br><b><br>D-17602-1:</b> My name is [REDACTED] and I'm 25 years old.<br><b><br>Dr. ██████:</b> Can you tell me what happened when you first came into contact with <i>SCP-17602</i>?<br><b><br>D-17602-1:</b> Well, I was walking down the street and I saw this girl. She was really pretty and she seemed to be looking at me. Then, out of nowhere, she started talking to me and flirting with me! It was like she couldn't help herself or something. We ended up going on a date that night and it's been like that ever since then.<br><br><h3> Addendum 17602.3: </h3> Experiment Log<br><b><br>Date:</b> ██/██/████<br><b><br>Subject:</b> D-17602-1, a 25 year old male human.<br><b><br>Procedure:</b> D-17602-1 was instructed to enter the testing chamber and make contact with <i>SCP-17602</i>. Upon contact, all female personnel within 30 meters of the subject were to be monitored for any changes in behavior or attitude towards the subject.<br><b><br>Results:</b> Upon making contact with <i>SCP-17602</i>, D-17602-1 experienced an immediate increase in attractiveness among female personnel within range. All females present began expressing strong feelings of attraction towards him and several even attempted to initiate romantic relationships with him before being restrained by security personnel. After two weeks of observation, the effects had faded away and returned back to normal levels.</div>
